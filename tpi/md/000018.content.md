## Sabis (id: 26218)

SABIS
=====

Wanpela man i gat olsem bikpela tru laik bilong wokim wok long mastam, na mastam i bai givim liklik laik bilong sekuriti. Ol samting man i bin olsem sklav long bihainim lo long liklik we olsem lejisles bondage; ol narapela olsem wokman ol i wokim long baim olgeta. I gat taim yu no inap lukim gut olsem 'sabis,' 'sklav,' 'bondman,' na 'bondwoman' i stap. Plenti wok long tok bilong dispela bok i bin bihainim olsem 'servant,' tasol ol niupela translesen i laikim narapela tok.

Tok bilong Hebrū long 'boi,' 'yuth,' o 'laiklikman' i save makim 'sabis' olgeta taim ([Ex 33:11](https://ref.ly/Exod33:11); [Nm 22:22](https://ref.ly/Num22:22); [2 Kgs 4:12](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:12)). Wok bilong toktok 'free\-born servant' i rilait long ol sabis bilong Givim Bikpela Man, olsem Levites ([Ezr 8:17](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:17); [Is 61:6](https://ref.ly/Isa61:6); [Ez 44:11](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:11)) o olsem preist ([Ex 28:35](https://ref.ly/Exod28:35);[Jl 1:9](https://ref.ly/Joel1:9); [2:17](https://ref.ly/Joel2:17)). Ol taim, ol minista bilong kwin ol i kolim olsem sabis ([1 Chr 27:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:1); [Prv 29:12](https://ref.ly/Prov29:12)), olsem tu ol angel i wokim wok bilong minista long pesin bilong Bikpela Man ([Pss 103:21](https://ref.ly/Ps103:21); [104:4](https://ref.ly/Ps104:4)). Hired sabis o ipla man we ol ipla baim long wok i bin konsida tu olsem man bilong fri ([Ex 12:45](https://ref.ly/Exod12:45); [Jb 7:1](https://ref.ly/Job7:1); [Mal 3:5](https://ref.ly/Mal3:5)).

Tok bilong Hebrū we i liklik tru long olgeta we i kamap olsem 800 taim insait long Ol Testament, i mekim olsem wanpela sklav we ol i holim long bel hevi ([Gn 9:25](https://ref.ly/Gen9:25); [12:16](https://ref.ly/Gen12:16); [Ex 20:17](https://ref.ly/Exod20:17); [Dt 5:15](https://ref.ly/Deut5:15); [15:17](https://ref.ly/Deut15:17)). Tasol, sapos bai yumi lukim tok i sem bilong yumi, i save yusim long ol manmeri we ol i gat bikpela rang, olsem minista na konsela long kwin ([2 Kgs 22:12](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:12); [2 Chr 34:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:20); [Neh 2:10](https://ref.ly/Neh2:10)) o olsem wanpela man bilong Bikpela Man ([Gn 24:14](https://ref.ly/Gen24:14); [Nm 12:7](https://ref.ly/Num12:7); [Jos 1:7](https://ref.ly/Josh1:7); [2 Kgs 21:8](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:8)), long tok olsem 'Moses \[o tu David, Isaiah, Israel, Job, etc.] bilong mi. Wanpela long ol nobol tok i 'sabis bilong Yahweh \[Bikpela Man]' ([Dt 34:5](https://ref.ly/Deut34:5); [Jos 1:13](https://ref.ly/Josh1:13); [8:31–33](https://ref.ly/Josh8:31-Josh8:33); [Is 49:1–6](https://ref.ly/Isa49:1-Isa49:6); [50:4–9](https://ref.ly/Isa50:4-Isa50:9); [52:13–53:12](https://ref.ly/Isa52:13-Isa53:12)). Nem Obadiah meri “sabis bilong Yahweh.”

Ol Niu Testamen i definim sabis long olgeta olsem sabis i yusim long wok o olsem yusim long baim long wok ([Mk 1:20](https://ref.ly/Mark1:20); [Lk 15:17–19](https://ref.ly/Luke15:17-Luke15:19); [Jn 10:12–14](https://ref.ly/John10:12-John10:14)), moa gut long dispela ol i definim sabis olsem sklav ([Mt 8:9](https://ref.ly/Matt8:9); [10:24–25](https://ref.ly/Matt10:24-Matt10:25); [13:27–28](https://ref.ly/Matt13:27-Matt13:28); [Mk 10:44](https://ref.ly/Mark10:44); [12:2–4](https://ref.ly/Mark12:2-Mark12:4); [Lk 7:2–3, 8–10](https://ref.ly/Luke7:2-Luke7:3,Luke7:8-Luke7:10); [Jn 4:51](https://ref.ly/John4:51); [8:34](https://ref.ly/John8:34); [13:16](https://ref.ly/John13:16); [Eph 6:5](https://ref.ly/Eph6:5); [Col 1:7](https://ref.ly/Col1:7)), na tu olsem domestic sabis ([Lk 16:13](https://ref.ly/Luke16:13)).

*Liklikbuk* i gat tu Slave na Slavery.
