## Bikpela (id: 26213)

BIKPELA
=======

“Bikpela” long English em i rendaring bilong Hibru ‘adonai o long Grik kurios*.* Long Hibru YHWH em i rendaring olsem “Bikpela”; *Lukim* Yahweh (YHWH).

Pawa na lidasip bilong God olsem Bikpela bilong yumi em i stap antap long kreisen na bosim olgeta samting na pipol ([Ps 24:1–2](https://ref.ly/Ps24:1-Ps24:2)). God ol i kolim em Bikpela long ol graun guria, win, paia ([1 Kgs 19:10–14](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:10-1Kgs19:14)), ol sta ([Is 40:26](https://ref.ly/Isa40:26)), ol bist na ol solwara monsta ([Jb 40–41](https://ref.ly/Job40:1-Job41:34)), na ol bagarap ([Pss 74:12–14](https://ref.ly/Ps74:12-Ps74:14); [89:8–10](https://ref.ly/Ps89:8-Ps89:10)), we i toktok long total supremasi long ol graun samting.

Ol lapun prope i soim olsem God i Bikpela Man o Bikpela King long long stori bilong ol man na kantri ([1 Raja 19:15–18](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:15-1Kgs19:18); [Isai 10:5–9](https://ref.ly/Isa10:5-Isa10:9); [Amos 9:7](https://ref.ly/Amos9:7)), na em i Bikpela Man bilong olgeta moroliti long olgeta man ([Ezekiel 25–32](https://ref.ly/Ezek25:1-Ezek32:32); [Amos 1:3–2:16](https://ref.ly/Amos1:3-Amos2:16)). Tasol em i wanpela bikpela Bikpela Man bilong Israel; olsem tok bilong en i soim wok na lain manmeri konstitusen bilong ol na ol i mas bihainim tru tru na tok bilong en na bihainim gut tru ([Exodus 20:2](https://ref.ly/Exod20:2)). Bikpela tiket bilong ol divin olsem i helpim Israel long taim ol i painim tru. Na ol i laikim long taim bipo, taim wanpela bikpela Dei bilong Bikpela Man bai kisim olgeta samting gut na bai paitim olgeta ol i bin rausim gut na bai bringim nambawan olsem bipo ([Isaia 2:2–4, 11–12](https://ref.ly/Isa2:2-Isa2:4,Isa2:11-Isa2:12); [34:8](https://ref.ly/Isa34:8); Ezekiel [30:1–5](https://ref.ly/Ezek30:1-Ezek30:5); Joel [2:31–3:1](https://ref.ly/Joel2:31-Joel3:1)).

Long Septuagint, ol liklik liklik wok “Lord/lord/master“ long tok Pisin i kurios*,* na long Greek NT i yusim tu bilong ol olsem mas, hapusen, na ol lain meri o maspla ([Matyu 25:11](https://ref.ly/Matt25:11); [Luk 14:21](https://ref.ly/Luke14:21); [Acts 25:26](https://ref.ly/Acts25:26); [1 Peter 3:6](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:6)); bilong God ([Matyu 11:25](https://ref.ly/Matt11:25); [Hebrew 8:2](https://ref.ly/Heb8:2)); na bilong ol foran god o deiti olsem bilong ol lain man ([1 Korinti 8:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:5)). I ol i yusim em tu long Jisas olsem wanpela liklik liklik tok bilong respekim ('sir,' [Matyu 8:2](https://ref.ly/Matt8:2); [15:25](https://ref.ly/Matt15:25)); em i saim olsem ol tok i bin yusim long Septuagint bilong tok lain meri o worship ([Matyu 3:3](https://ref.ly/Matt3:3); [Luk 7:13](https://ref.ly/Luke7:13); [Acts 5:14](https://ref.ly/Acts5:14); [9:10](https://ref.ly/Acts9:10); [1 Korinti 6:13–14](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:13-1Cor6:14); [Heb 2:3](https://ref.ly/Heb2:3); [Jakob 5:7](https://ref.ly/Jas5:7)); I em i soim long ol toktok olsem 'the Lord Jesus,' 'the Lord’s Day,' 'the Lord’s Table,' 'the Spirit of the Lord' (we i tu em 'Lord,'[2 Korinti 3:17](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:17)), 'long Lord,' 'from the Lord,' 'light long Lord,' 'boast long Lord. Long ol taim olsem i no klia wok long tupela God o Jisas em i laik tokautim em, ([Acts 9:31](https://ref.ly/Acts9:31); [2 Korinti 8:21](https://ref.ly/2Cor8:21)). Em titil i tokaut long Jisas long olsem em i tokim long Jisas em istap long [Jon 13:13–14](https://ref.ly/John13:13-John13:14); na long [Jon 20:28](https://ref.ly/John20:28), Jisas i akseptim em olsem 'Masta bilong mi na God' bilong mi!'

Long wanpela Christian sarimoni emi mekim Jisas bikpela tokaut long olsem olsem bilong ol i save holim bel bilong salim ([Acts 2:21](https://ref.ly/Acts2:21)). Em i lukluk olsem ol lain i tokim long pablik taim ol i tokautim Jisas olsem Masta i bin aprovim focus na tokim bilong Christian bilong pilim, na i bin basis bilong membership long apostolic bikpela church ([Acts 16:31](https://ref.ly/Acts16:31); [Rom 10:9](https://ref.ly/Rom10:9); [1 Korinti 12:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:3); [Filipai 2:11](https://ref.ly/Phil2:11)). Tasol, i save kamap moa bikpela tok i mas bihainim olsem wanpela formal tok olsem wanpela insia bilong tokaut long tok—olsem na ol tok nogut long [Matyu 7:21](https://ref.ly/Matt7:21) na [Luk 6:46](https://ref.ly/Luke6:46) i save tokautim.

Long tasol, tokautim olsem wanpela bekpela tok i bin kamap wantaim bikpela tingting. Long ol tok ples olsem, 'lord' em i tokaut long sistem bilong sklaim na i tokaut long masta i gat tru tru bikpela pasin bilong kontrol long sklaim husat i bin painim na baim ol. Paul i no gat nating i stopim, em i tokautim gut ol gutpela tingting bilong redemption bilong Christian long ([1 Korinti 6:19–20](https://ref.ly/1Cor6:19-1Cor6:20) na [7:22–23](https://ref.ly/1Cor7:22-1Cor7:23);*lukim* foto). Long dok bilong ol Juda, dispela titil i bin gat olsem olsem wari bilong mesianik long kingship na authority ([Luk 20:41–44](https://ref.ly/Luke20:41-Luke20:44)), na em i mekim ol Juda na Rom ol i no likim. Politikali, "Masta" em wanpela titil i bin dai bilong Caesar. Istap long Domitian taim we Caesar worship i olsem kain wok, em i bikpela tingting olsem Jisas i bin kolim "King bilong ol king na Masta bilong ol masta" ([Rivelasen 17:14](https://ref.ly/Rev17:14); [19:16](https://ref.ly/Rev19:16)).

Long ol Juda ol i save toktok Greek na olsem ol Juda long ol narapela hap bilong dispela i save yusim tok "Masta" long Jisas em i laik longpela tingting, bikpela kas tru, bikpela hol, bikpela sin tru. Bikpela toktok em oli yusim long tokautim ol narapela deiti bilong ol olsem ol olsem ol i gods bilong polytheism. Em i save tru, bikos "Masta" em i save taim em i kamap wantaim "Man bilong God," pray, tingting olgeta nogut, bikpela tingting, na hop olgeta ([1 Korinti 8:5–6](https://ref.ly/1Cor8:5-1Cor8:6); [Filipai 2:9–11](https://ref.ly/Phil2:9-Phil2:11); [1 Tesalonika 4:14–17](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:14-1Thess4:17)). Long olgeta lain o liklik lain, bikpela tingting em oli tokim long Jisas em oli givim hamas na i no onli gat tok long spirit, tasol i gat bikpela bikpela bikpela bikpela danger tu.

*Lukim tu* Kristoloji; God, Wok na Nait bilong en; God, Nem bilong en.
