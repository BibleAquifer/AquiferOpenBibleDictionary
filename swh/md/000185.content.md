## Mahalaleel, Mahalaleli (id: 800602)

MAHALALEEL, MAHALELI
====================

1. Mwana wa Kenani na baba wa Yaredi katika ukoo wa Sethi ([Mwanzo 5:12–17](https://ref.ly/Gen5:12-Gen5:17); [1 Mambo ya Nyakati 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:2)). Pia anatajwa katika [Luka 3:37](https://ref.ly/Luke3:37).

    *Tazama* Ukoo wa Yesu Kristo.

2. Mwana wa Peresi na mshiriki wa kabila la Yuda aliyeishi baada ya Wayahudi kurudi kutoka uhamishoni Babeli ([Nehemia 11:4](https://ref.ly/Neh11:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 1:2; NEH 11:4; LUK 3:37; GEN 5:12–GEN 5:17
