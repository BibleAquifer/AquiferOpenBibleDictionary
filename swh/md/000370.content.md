## Thadayo, Mtume (id: 468219)

THADAYO, Mtume
==============

Mmoja wa mitume 12 wa asili kulingana na orodha katika [Marko 3:18](https://ref.ly/Mark3:18) na [Mathayo 10:3](https://ref.ly/Matt10:3) (kjv "Lebayo, ambaye jina lake la pili lilikuwa Thadayo"). Inawezekana kabisa kwamba huyu ni mtu yule yule kama Yuda mwana wa Yakobo (sio Iskariote) katika [Luka 6:16](https://ref.ly/Luke6:16) na [Matendo 1:13](https://ref.ly/Acts1:13). *Tazama* Mtume, Utume.

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 10:3; MRK 3:18; LUK 6:16; ACT 1:13
