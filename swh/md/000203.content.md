## Mateso (id: 800623)

MATESO
======

Kitu chochote kinachosababisha maumivu au dhiki, kama vile majanga.

Kulingana na Biblia, mateso yalianza wakati dhambi ilipoingia ulimwenguni. Binadamu na viumbe vyote vilikumbwa na "miiba na mibaruti," dhambi, kifo, na uharibifu (linganisha [Mwanzo 3:16–19](https://ref.ly/Gen3:16-Gen3:19); [Warumi 8:18–21](https://ref.ly/Rom8:18-Rom8:21)). Kwa sababu ya dhambi, mateso ni sehemu ya kawaida ya maisha ya binadamu, na maisha yetu mafupi yamejaa matatizo ([Ayubu 14:1–6](https://ref.ly/Job14:1-Job14:6)). Watu hawawezi kuepuka majanga ya asili, majeraha ya kimwili, na migogoro na wengine ([2 Mambo ya Nyakati 20:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:9)). Hata hivyo, Mungu hutumia mateso kufundisha na kuadhibu watu wake. Mateso haya yanaonyeshwa na ukandamizaji ambao Waisraeli walikumbana nao katika:

* Misri ([Kutoka 4:31](https://ref.ly/Exod4:31))
* Vita vyao wakati wa Waamuzi ([Nehemia 9:26–27](https://ref.ly/Neh9:26-Neh9:27))
* Uhamisho wao huko Babeli ([Isaya 26:16](https://ref.ly/Isa26:16))

Katika dhiki zao, Waisraeli walimlilia Mungu. Aliwaokoa na kuwaongoza kutii ([Yeremia 10:18](https://ref.ly/Jer10:18); [Hosea 5:15–6:3](https://ref.ly/Hos5:15-Hos6:3)).

Biblia inatambua kuwa ni vigumu kuelewa. Ni vigumu kuelewa kwa nini wenye haki wanateseka sana ([Zaburi 34:19](https://ref.ly/Ps34:19); [37:39](https://ref.ly/Ps37:39); [138:7](https://ref.ly/Ps138:7)). Hata nabii na “Mtumishi wa Bwana” (Masihi) hawakuepuka mateso ([Isaya 53:2–12](https://ref.ly/Isa53:2-Isa53:12); [Yeremia 15:15](https://ref.ly/Jer15:15)). Yesu Kristo alibeba huzuni na majonzi ya wanadamu. Alitimiza unabii wa mateso ulioanza na dhambi ya Adamu ([Isaya 53:4–5](https://ref.ly/Isa53:4-Isa53:5); [1 Petro 2:24](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:24)).

Yesu alionya kwamba wafuasi wake wangekabili majaribu mengi na huzuni ([Yohana 16:33](https://ref.ly/John16:33)). Paulo alifundisha kwamba kuingia katika ufalme wa Mungu kunahusisha matatizo mengi ([Matendo 14:22](https://ref.ly/Acts14:22)). Hata hivyo, haya hayapaswi kudhoofisha imani ya Mkristo ([1 Thessalonians 3:3](https://ref.ly/1Thess3:3)). Yanapaswa kuonekana kama kukamilisha kile kinachokosekana katika mateso ya Kristo kwa ajili ya mwili wake, kanisa ([2 Corinthians 4:10–11](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:10-2Cor4:11); [Wakolosai 1:24](https://ref.ly/Col1:24)). Biblia pia inapendekeza kwamba mateso yatakuwa makali zaidi kadiri "mwisho" unavyokaribia ([Mathayo 24:9–14](https://ref.ly/Matt24:9-Matt24:14); [2 Timotheo 3:13](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:13)). Nguvu za Shetani zitashambulia ili kudanganya na kuharibu "wateule" ([Mathayo 24:24](https://ref.ly/Matt24:24); [2 Thessalonians 2:9–12](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:9-2Thess2:12); [Ufunuo 20:7–9](https://ref.ly/Rev20:7-Rev20:9)). Yesu Kristo atakapofunuliwa kutoka mbinguni katika moto wa kuwaka, Mungu atawatesa wale waliowadhuru waumini. Atalipiza kisasi kwa wale ambao hawakutii injili ya Yesu Kristo ([Warumi 2:9](https://ref.ly/Rom2:9); [2 Thessalonians 1:5–10](https://ref.ly/2Thess1:5-2Thess1:10); [2:7–8](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:7-2Thess2:8)).

*Tazama pia* Mateso; Shida.
