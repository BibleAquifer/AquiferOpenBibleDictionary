## Yairo (id: 713711)

Yairo
=====

Kiongozi wa sinagogi, labda huko Kapernaumu, alimkuta Yesu katika umati na kumwomba amponye binti yake aliyekuwa karibu kufa. Wakati Yesu alicheleweshwa na uponyaji mwingine, Yairo alijulishwa kuwa binti yake alikuwa amekufa. Yesu alimhimiza asiogope bali aamini. Kisha Yesu akaenda nyumbani kwa Yairo, akawafukuza waombolezaji, na kumrudisha msichana huyo kwenye uhai ([Marko 5:22, 35–42](https://ref.ly/Mark5:22,Mark5:35-Mark5:42); [Luka 8:41, 49–55](https://ref.ly/Luke8:41,Luke8:49-Luke8:55)).

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 5:22; LUK 8:41; MRK 5:35–MRK 5:42; LUK 8:49–LUK 8:55
