## Huzuni (id: 713700)

HUZUNI
======

Mateso ya kihisia yanayosababishwa na kupoteza, bahati mbaya, au janga. Kuomboleza kunamaanisha kuhisi huzuni au dhiki.

Ni Mifano Ipi ya Huzuni Inayopatikana Katika Biblia?
----------------------------------------------------

Wazo la huzuni linajitokeza mara nyingi katika Biblia:

* Isaka na Rebeka walihuzunika wakati mwana wao Esau alipooa mwanamke Mhiti ([Mwanzo 26:35](https://ref.ly/Gen26:35))
* Mungu alihuzunika kwa maumivu ambayo Waisraeli walijiletea kwa kutotii kwao ([Waamuzi 10:16](https://ref.ly/Judg10:16))
* Hana alikuwa na huzuni kwa sababu hakuwa na mtoto, kiasi kwamba alionekana kama mlevi wakati wa kuomba ([1 Samweli 1:16](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:16))
* Samweli aliomba usiku kucha kwa sababu alihuzunishwa na kutotii kwa Mfalme Sauli ([1 Samweli 15:11](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:11))
* Ayubu alikuwa na huzuni kubwa sana kuhusu hasara yake binafsi ([Ayubu 2:13](https://ref.ly/Job2:13); linganisha [6:2](https://ref.ly/Job6:2); [16:6](https://ref.ly/Job16:6))
* Waandishi wa zaburi walionyesha dhiki na huzuni ([Zaburi 6:7](https://ref.ly/Ps6:7); [31:9](https://ref.ly/Ps31:9-Ps31:10)–[10](https://ref.ly/Ps31:9-Ps31:10); [69:26](https://ref.ly/Ps69:26); [73:21](https://ref.ly/Ps73:21); [95:10](https://ref.ly/Ps95:10); [112:10](https://ref.ly/Ps112:10))
* Kitabu cha Maombolezo kimejitolea kwa kuonyesha huzuni
* Manabii huzungumzia hukumu inayotokana na Israeli kumhuzunisha Mungu
* Yesu alipata huzuni na dhiki, ikiwa ni pamoja na kulia kwa kifo cha rafiki yake ([Marko 3:5](https://ref.ly/Mark3:5); [Yohana 11:33](https://ref.ly/John11:33); [35](https://ref.ly/John11:35))
* Wayahudi walihuzunika wakati mitume walipokuwa wakifundisha kuhusu Kristo ([Matendo 4:2](https://ref.ly/Acts4:2))
* Mtume Paulo aliwafundisha waumini wasihuzunishane ([Warumi 14:15](https://ref.ly/Rom14:15))
* Mtume Paulo hakutaka kusababisha huzuni yoyote yeye mwenyewe ([2 Wakorintho 2:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor2:1-2Cor2:5)–[5](https://ref.ly/2Cor2:1-2Cor2:5))
* Muumini hapaswi kumhuzunisha Roho Mtakatifu ([Waefeso 4:30](https://ref.ly/Eph4:30))
* Muumini anaweza kupitia huzuni na mateso katika ulimwengu usio wa kawaida ([1 Petro 2:19](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:19))
* Huzuni ilionyeshwa wakati wa kifo kupitia kilio, maombolezo, na vilio ([Yeremia 9:17](https://ref.ly/Jer9:17-Jer9:18)–[18](https://ref.ly/Jer9:17-Jer9:18); [Amosi 5:16](https://ref.ly/Amos5:16); [Marko 5:38](https://ref.ly/Mark5:38)).

*Tazama pia* Ombolezo.
