## Muhunzi (id: 800593)

MUHUNZI\*
=========

Fundimiani ambaye kawaida alifanya kazi na chuma ([Is 44:12](https://ref.ly/Isa44:12)); fundi chuma. Mfanyakazi wa kwanza wa chuma aliyerekodiwa katika Biblia ni Tubali\-kaini ([Gn 4:22](https://ref.ly/Gen4:22)). Katika Israeli, chuma kilijulikana sana na kilitumika takriban karne ya 11 KK ([Dt 3:11](https://ref.ly/Deut3:11); [Jos 6:19, 24](https://ref.ly/Josh6:19,Josh6:24); [17:16](https://ref.ly/Josh17:16); [Jgs 1:19](https://ref.ly/Judg1:19); [4:3, 13](https://ref.ly/Judg4:3,Judg4:13)). *Tazama* Madini na Metali.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 4:22; DEU 3:11; JOS 6:19; JOS 6:24; JOS 17:16; JDG 1:19; JDG 4:3; JDG 4:13; ISA 44:12
* **Associated Articles:** Madini na Metali (ID: `800607@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
