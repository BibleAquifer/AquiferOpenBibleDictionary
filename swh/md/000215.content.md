## Mchungaji (id: 713740)

Mchungaji
=========

Mtu ambaye alibeba jukumu kamili la kundi la kondoo. Kazi yake ilikuwa kutafuta nyasi na maji kwa ajili ya kondoo, kuwalinda dhidi ya wanyama wa porini ([Amosi 3:12](https://ref.ly/Amos3:12)), kutafuta na kurudisha wale waliopotea ([Ezekieli 34:8](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:8); [Mathayo 18:12](https://ref.ly/Matt18:12)), kuongoza kundi kutoka zizini kila siku, na kurudisha kundi zizini mwishoni mwa siku ([Yohana 10:2–4](https://ref.ly/John10:2-John10:4)).

Fumbo la mchungaji na kondoo wake ni muhimu katika Agano Jipya. Yesu ni Mchungaji Mwema ambaye anatoa maisha yake kwa ajili ya kondoo ([Mathayo 18:10–14](https://ref.ly/Matt18:10-Matt18:14); [Marko 6:34](https://ref.ly/Mark6:34); [Yohana 10](https://ref.ly/John10:1-John10:42); [Waebrania 13:20](https://ref.ly/Heb13:20)). Mfano wa mchungaji na kundi lake unapata maelezo bora katika [Zaburi 23](https://ref.ly/Ps23:1-Ps23:6), [Ezekieli 34](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:1-Ezek34:31), na [Yohana 10](https://ref.ly/John10:1-John10:42). Mungu alikuwa Mchungaji wa Israeli ([Mwanzo 49:24](https://ref.ly/Gen49:24); [Zaburi 23:1](https://ref.ly/Ps23:1); [80:1](https://ref.ly/Ps80:1); [Isaya 40:11](https://ref.ly/Isa40:11)). Wakati wachungaji wasio waaminifu waliposhindwa Israeli, Mungu aliingilia kati na kumteua mtumishi wake Daudi kama mchungaji mwaminifu juu yao ([Ezekieli 34:11–16, 23–24](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:11-Ezek34:16,Ezek34:23-Ezek34:24)).

Picha za Agano Jipya zinatokana na Agano la Kale na asili ya Palestina. Katika uchumi wa Kiyahudi, mchungaji aliyekuwa akichunga kundi la kondoo au mbuzi alikuwa na jukumu la uwajibikaji. Makundi makubwa yalihitaji kuhamishwa kutoka sehemu moja hadi nyingine na pia yalihitaji kulindwa dhidi ya wanyama wakali na wanyang'anyi. Kwa sababu ya jukumu la msingi la uchungaji katika ulimwengu wa kale, neno "mchungaji" likawa neno la kawaida kwa mtawala. Wafalme wa Ashuru, Babuloni, na Misri mara nyingi walirejelewa kama wachungaji waliowalinda watu wao. Picha hii iliunda msingi wa Agano la Kale, ambapo matumizi hayo hayo yanapatikana. Mungu anaonyeshwa kama mchungaji wa Israeli, anayejali kila kipengele cha ustawi wa watu wake. Watawala na viongozi wa watu mara nyingi wanarejelewa kama wachungaji ([Hesabu 27:17](https://ref.ly/Num27:17); [1 Wafalme 22:17](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:17); [Yeremia 10:21](https://ref.ly/Jer10:21); [12:10](https://ref.ly/Jer12:10); [22:22](https://ref.ly/Jer22:22); [23:1–2](https://ref.ly/Jer23:1-Jer23:2)).

Wakati wa Yeremia, "mchungaji" lilianza kutumiwa kama jina la Masihi ajaye. Mungu mwenyewe angewatunza kondoo wake ([Yeremia 23:3](https://ref.ly/Jer23:3); [31:10](https://ref.ly/Jer31:10); [Ezekieli 34:11–22](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:11-Ezek34:22)) na aliahidi kutoa wachungaji waaminifu ambao wangeonyesha kujali watu wake ([Yeremia 3:15](https://ref.ly/Jer3:15); [23:4](https://ref.ly/Jer23:4)). Aliahidi wazi kwamba angekuwa Mungu wao na angeweka Mwana wa Daudi wa kimasihi kama mchungaji wao ([Ezekieli 34:23–24](https://ref.ly/Ezek34:23-Ezek34:24)). Katika Agano Jipya, Yesu alijitambulisha kama Mchungaji wa kimasihi aliyeahidiwa ([Mathayo 10:16](https://ref.ly/Matt10:16); [25:32](https://ref.ly/Matt25:32); [Marko 14:27](https://ref.ly/Mark14:27); [Yohana 10:1–30](https://ref.ly/John10:1-John10:30); linganisha [Waebrania 13:20](https://ref.ly/Heb13:20); [1 Petro 2:25](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:25)).

[Waefeso 4:11](https://ref.ly/Eph4:11) inazungumzia viongozi wa kanisa kama wachungaji. Matumizi haya yaliendelea katika kanisa la awali na yanaendelea hadi leo. Paulo alisema wao ni watu maalum waliotolewa kwa kanisa na Mungu ili kuwajali watu wa Mungu kama mchungaji anavyofanya kwa kondoo wake, kuwaongoza na kuwafundisha katika njia za Mungu. Petro pia alizungumza kuhusu viongozi kama wachungaji. Aliwahimiza kubaki wachungaji waaminifu hadi wakati ambapo Jemadari Mchungaji, Yesu Kristo, atakapotokea ([1 Petro 5:1–4](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:1-1Pet5:4)).
