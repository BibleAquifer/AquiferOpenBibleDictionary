## Kristo (id: 689566)

Kristo
======

Kichwa rasmi ambacho watu walitumia kwa Yesu katika Agano Jipya kinaonyesha jukumu lake kama Mwokozi aliyetiwa mafuta na kinarejelea sifa zake za kiroho za kuwaokoa watu wake.

"Kristo" Linamaanisha Nini?
---------------------------

Neno linatokana na neno la Kigiriki *Christos.* *Christos* ni tafsiri ya Kigiriki ya neno la Kiebrania Masihi ([Yohana 1:41](https://ref.ly/John1:41)). Maneno yote mawili yanatokana na vitenzi vinavyomaanisha “kupaka mafuta matakatifu” na kama vyeo yanamaanisha “Aliyetiwa Mafuta.” Watu wanapomwita Yesu "Kristo," wanaonyesha imani kwamba Mungu alimchagua kwa ajili ya jukumu na kazi yake.

Yesu na Wengine Walitumiaje Jina "Kristo"?
------------------------------------------

Kwenye Agano Jipya, neno "kichwa" linatumika kwa njia tofauti:

* ikiwa pamoja na jina la Yesu, kama “Yesu Kristo” ([Mathayo 1:1](https://ref.ly/Matt1:1); [Marko 1:1](https://ref.ly/Mark1:1); [Warumi 1:4](https://ref.ly/Rom1:4)) au “Kristo Yesu” ([Warumi 1:1](https://ref.ly/Rom1:1); [1 Wakorintho 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:1))
* na kifungu "the" ([Warumi 7:4](https://ref.ly/Rom7:4))
* na jina lingine kama "Bwana" ([Warumi 16:18](https://ref.ly/Rom16:18))
* pekee kama jina lingine linalopendekezwa au cheo kwa Yesu ([Yohana 20:31](https://ref.ly/John20:31); [Warumi 15:3](https://ref.ly/Rom15:3); [Waebrania 3:6](https://ref.ly/Heb3:6); [5:5](https://ref.ly/Heb5:5); [1 Petro 1:11, 19](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:11,1Pet1:19))

Injili zinaonyesha Yesu akipokea kwa unyenyekevu cheo na jukumu la Masihi. Ubatizo wake unaonyesha kwamba Mungu alimchagua kwa kazi tatu muhimu:

* kutoa ujumbe wa Mungu (nabii)
* Kuwasaidia watu kumkaribia Mungu (kuhani)
* kuwaongoza watu wa Mungu (mfalme)

Wakati wa ubatizo wake na Yohana (Eliya mpya, [Mathayo 11:14](https://ref.ly/Matt11:14)), Roho Mtakatifu alishuka juu ya Yesu na Mungu alimwambia aanze huduma yake ([Mathayo 3:16–4:17](https://ref.ly/Matt3:16-Matt4:17)). Yohana mwenyewe alikana kuwa yeye ndiye mteule lakini alipendekeza kwamba Yesu ndiye Kristo ([Yohana 1:20](https://ref.ly/John1:20); [Luka 3:14–17](https://ref.ly/Luke3:14-Luke3:17)).

Wanafunzi wa kwanza wa Yesu walimfuata kwa sababu walijua alikuwa Masihi ([Yohana 1:41](https://ref.ly/John1:41)). Pepo walimtambua kama “Mtakatifu \[aliyepakwa mafuta] wa Mungu” ([Marko 1:24](https://ref.ly/Mark1:24); linganisha [Mathayo 8:29](https://ref.ly/Matt8:29)). Umati ulimfuata kama nabii, Mose mpya ([Yohana 6:14, 32](https://ref.ly/John6:14,John6:32)). Hata hivyo, walimwacha walipoelewa kwamba ufalme wake ulikuwa wa kiroho, si wa kisiasa (sura ya [66](https://ref.ly/John6:66)). Wale Kumi na Wawili walibaki waaminifu, wakisema, “Tunaamini . . . Wewe ni Mtakatifu wa Mungu” ([Yohana 6:69](https://ref.ly/John6:69)). Ukiri wa wanafunzi uliosemwa na Petro na kuidhinishwa na Yesu kama Ufunuo wa kimungu (ujumbe kutoka kwa Mungu) ni “Wewe ni Kristo, Mwana wa Mungu aliye hai” ([Mathayo 16:16](https://ref.ly/Matt16:16)). Katika majaribio yake, dai la Yesu kuwa Kristo lilikuwa sababu kuu ya kuhukumiwa kwake ([Mathayo 26:63–64, 68](https://ref.ly/Matt26:63-Matt26:64,Matt26:68); [27:11, 17, 22, 37](https://ref.ly/Matt27:11,Matt27:17,Matt27:22,Matt27:37)).

Sehemu muhimu ya mahubiri ya kwanza ya Kikristo ilikuwa kutangaza kwamba Yesu ni Kristo ([Matendo 2:36](https://ref.ly/Acts2:36); [3:18–20](https://ref.ly/Acts3:18-Acts3:20); [9:22](https://ref.ly/Acts9:22); [28:23, 31](https://ref.ly/Acts28:23,Acts28:31)). Hii inabaki kuwa makala ya kwanza na ya msingi zaidi ya ungamo la Kikristo ([Mathayo 16:16](https://ref.ly/Matt16:16)) ([1 Wakorintho 1:23](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:23); [1 Yohana 5:1](https://ref.ly/1John5:1)). Inathibitisha kwamba Yesu alitimiza kikamilifu jukumu la nabii, kuhani, na mfalme aliyechaguliwa kama mtumishi wa Mungu kwa watu wake ([Luka 7:16](https://ref.ly/Luke7:16); [1 Wakorintho 15:25](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:25); [Waebrania 7:22–28](https://ref.ly/Heb7:22-Heb7:28); [Ufunuo 19:16](https://ref.ly/Rev19:16)).

*tazama pia* Yesu Kristo, Maisha na Mafundisho yake; Masihi.
