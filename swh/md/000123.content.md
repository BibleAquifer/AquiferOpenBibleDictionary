## Kenani (id: 800598)

KENANI
======

Mzao wa Adamu. Aliishi vizazi vinne baada ya Adamu, akiwa mwana wa Enoshi ([Mwanzo 5:9–14](https://ref.ly/Gen5:9-Gen5:14); [1 Mambo ya Nyakati 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:2)). Pia anajulikana kama Kenani katika [Luka 3:37](https://ref.ly/Luke3:37).

*Tazama* Ukoo wa Yesu Kristo.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 1:2; LUK 3:37; GEN 5:9–GEN 5:14
