## Maomboleza (id: 713722)

MAOMBOLEZO
==========

Huu ni utaratibu wa mazoea ambayo watu hufuata mtu anapofariki. Mazoea haya hufuatwa na familia na marafiki wa marehemu.

Maombolezo katika Agano la Kale
-------------------------------

Ilikuwa ni kufunga macho ya wafu, kukumbatia mwili, na kuandaa kwa mazishi ([Mwanzo 46:4](https://ref.ly/Gen46:4); [50:1](https://ref.ly/Gen50:1)). Mazishi ya haraka yalihitajika kutokana na hali ya hewa ya joto ([Matendo 5:1–10](https://ref.ly/Acts5:1-Acts5:10)). Hatuna maelezo mengi kuhusu desturi za mazishi kabla ya Agano Jipya ([Mathayo 27:59](https://ref.ly/Matt27:59); [Yohana 11:44](https://ref.ly/John11:44); [19:39–40](https://ref.ly/John19:39-John19:40)). Ushahidi wa akiolojia unapendekeza kwamba watu walizikwa wakiwa wamevaa nguo kamili na si kwenye majeneza. Waisraeli hawakuwaka au kuchoma wafu wao, lakini mazishi sahihi yalikuwa muhimu sana.

Wakati mtu alipofariki, ilikuwa kawaida kufanya:

* Kuchana nguo za Mtu ([Mwanzo 37:34](https://ref.ly/Gen37:34); [2 Samweli 1:11](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:11); [Ayubu 1:20](https://ref.ly/Job1:20))
* Kuvaa magunia ([2 Samweli 3:31](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:31))
* Kuvua viatu vya mtu ([2 Samweli 15:30](https://ref.ly/2Sam15:30); [Mika 1:8](https://ref.ly/Mic1:8)) na mavazi ya kichwani.
* Mtu anaweza kufunika ndevu zake au uso wake ([Ezekieli 24:17, 23](https://ref.ly/Ezek24:17,Ezek24:23))
* Waombolezaji waliweka udongo juu ya vichwa vyao ([Yoshua 7:6](https://ref.ly/Josh7:6); [1 Samweli 4:12](https://ref.ly/1Sam4:12); [Nehemia 9:1](https://ref.ly/Neh9:1); [Ayubu 2:12](https://ref.ly/Job2:12); [Ezekieli 27:30](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:30))
* Walijiviringisha katika mavumbi ([Ayubu 16:15](https://ref.ly/Job16:15); [Mika 1:10](https://ref.ly/Mic1:10))
* Alikaa juu ya lundo la majivu ([Esta 4:3](https://ref.ly/Esth4:3); [Isaya 58:5](https://ref.ly/Isa58:5); [Yeremia 6:26](https://ref.ly/Jer6:26); [Ezekieli 27:30](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:30))

Baadhi ya desturi za maombolezo zilipigwa marufuku kwa sababu zilikuwa za kipagani ([Mambo ya Walawi 19:27–28](https://ref.ly/Lev19:27-Lev19:28); [Kumbukumbu la Torati 14:1](https://ref.ly/Deut14:1)):

* Kukata nywele na kunyoa ndevu
* Kujikata mwilini ([Ayubu 1:20](https://ref.ly/Job1:20); [Isaya 22:12](https://ref.ly/Isa22:12); [Yeremia 16:6](https://ref.ly/Jer16:6); [41:5](https://ref.ly/Jer41:5); [47:5](https://ref.ly/Jer47:5); [48:37](https://ref.ly/Jer48:37); [Ezekieli 7:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:18); [Amosi 8:10](https://ref.ly/Amos8:10))

Watu waliokuwa katika maombolezo waliacha kuoga na kutumia manukato ([2 Samweli 12:20](https://ref.ly/2Sam12:20); [14:2](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:2)).

Kufunga pia ilikuwa desturi ya kawaida ya maombolezo ([1 Samweli 31:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam31:13); [2 Samweli 1:12](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:12)). Majirani au marafiki wangeleta mkate wa maombolezo na "kikombe cha faraja" kwa jamaa wa marehemu ([Yeremia 16:7](https://ref.ly/Jer16:7); [Ezekieli 24:17, 22](https://ref.ly/Ezek24:17,Ezek24:22)). Hii ilifanyika kwa sababu chakula hakiwezi kuandaliwa nyumbani kwa marehemu kutokana na uchafu wa kiibada. Marehemu walichukuliwa kuwa wachafu kiasi kwamba makuhani wangeweza tu kushiriki katika maombolezo kwa jamaa zao wa karibu wa damu (mama, baba, mwana, binti, kaka, na dada, mradi tu alikuwa bado bikira; [Mambo ya Walawi 21:1](https://ref.ly/Lev21:1-Lev21:4,Lev21:10-Lev21:11)[–](https://ref.ly/Lev21:1-Lev21:4)[4, 10](https://ref.ly/Lev21:1-Lev21:4,Lev21:10-Lev21:11)[–](https://ref.ly/Lev21:1-Lev21:4)[11](https://ref.ly/Lev21:1-Lev21:4,Lev21:10-Lev21:11)). Maombolezo yalikuwa maonyesho ya huzuni na mapenzi badala ya matendo ya ibada yaliyolengwa kwa marehemu au kuunda ibada kwa ajili ya marehemu.

Kaburini, maombolezo kwa wafu yalikuwa ya kawaida ([1 Wafalme 13:30](https://ref.ly/1Kgs13:30); [Yeremia 6:26](https://ref.ly/Jer6:26); [Amosi 5:16](https://ref.ly/Amos5:16); [8:10](https://ref.ly/Amos8:10); [Zekaria 12:10](https://ref.ly/Zech12:10)). Wanaume na wanawake waliomboleza katika makundi tofauti ([Zekaria 12:11–14](https://ref.ly/Zech12:11-Zech12:14)). Maombolezo haya ya huzuni yangeweza kuendelea kuwa maombolezo ya kimuziki ([2 Samweli 1:17–27](https://ref.ly/2Sam1:17-2Sam1:27); [Amosi 8:10](https://ref.ly/Amos8:10)). Waombolezaji wa kitaalamu, hasa wanawake, mara nyingi waliandikwa kuongoza maombolezo ([Yeremia 9:17–19](https://ref.ly/Jer9:17-Jer9:19); [Amosi 5:16](https://ref.ly/Amos5:16)). Kitabu cha Maombolezo kinaonyesha aina hii. Maombolezo yanatukumbusha kwamba kuomboleza hakukuwa tu kuhusiana na kifo bali pia kuonyesha kuvunjika kwa roho kwa dhambi, za mtu binafsi na za kitaifa.

Ibada hizi za maombolezo zilionyesha huzuni kubwa. Baadhi ya desturi, kama kurarua nguo, kuvaa magunia, kujifunika kwa vumbi na majivu, na hata kujikata, zilionyesha huzuni kali. Maana ya kidini ya desturi hizi sasa haieleweki kwetu. Maombolezo hayakuwa tu hisia za ndani au hali ya kiakili; yalikuwa ni ibada iliyokusudiwa na iliyoanzishwa. Mtu alipokufa, Mwisraeli alilia kwa sababu ilikuwa ni desturi na sahihi. Watu walijenga makaburi au kumbukumbu ([2 Samweli 18:18](https://ref.ly/2Sam18:18)). Hata hivyo, Waisraeli wengi walikuwa maskini sana kwa hili kuwa desturi ya kawaida.

Maombolezo katika Agano Jipya
-----------------------------

Desturi za kuomboleza katika Agano Jipya zilifanana na zile za Agano la Kale. Kuomboleza kulihusisha:

* Kujili kwa Pili kwa Kristo ([Mathayo 24:30](https://ref.ly/Matt24:30))
* Toba ([Yakobo 4:8–10](https://ref.ly/Jas4:8-Jas4:10))
* Kristo Akiwacha 12 ([Mathayo 9:15](https://ref.ly/Matt9:15))
* Kina kiroho ([5:4](https://ref.ly/Matt5:4))
* Kifo ([Marko 5:38–39](https://ref.ly/Mark5:38-Mark5:39); [Luka 7:13](https://ref.ly/Luke7:13); [Yohana 11:33](https://ref.ly/John11:33))

Ingawa Wakristo waliamini kwamba ufufuo wa Kristo ulikuwa umeshinda kifo ([1 Wakorintho 15:54–57](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:54-1Cor15:57)), bado waliomboleza, lakini si kama wale wasio na tumaini ([1 Wathesalonike 4:13](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:13); [Ufunuo 21:4](https://ref.ly/Rev21:4)).

*Tazama pia* Mazishi, Mila za Mazishi; Mila za Mazishi.
