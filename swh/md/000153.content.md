## Kupiga magoti (id: 689591)

KUPIGA MAGOTI
=============

Nafasi ya mwili inayomaanisha kumwabudu, kumheshimu, au kujisalimisha kwa mtu.

Magoti yenye nguvu yaliwakilisha imani thabiti ya mtu, hivyo kupiga magoti kulionyesha heshima kwa mtu mwenye mamlaka zaidi. Watu walipiga magoti mbele ya wafalme, watawala, magavana, au Mungu. Katika [Mwanzo 41:43](https://ref.ly/Gen41:43), watu walipiga magoti mbele ya Farao na Yosefu. Watu walionyesha heshima yao kwa Mungu kwa kupiga magoti ([Isaya 45:23](https://ref.ly/Isa45:23); [Warumi 14:11](https://ref.ly/Rom14:11); [Wafilipi 2:10](https://ref.ly/Phil2:10)). Wakati wa njaa ambapo hakukuwa na chakula cha kutosha, baadhi ya Waisraeli walimwacha Mungu. Wale waliobaki waaminifu waliitwa “wale wote ambao magoti yao hayajapigiwa Baali” ([1 Wafalme 19:18](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:18); tazama [Warumi 11:4](https://ref.ly/Rom11:4)).

Magoti imara yalionyesha nguvu, hivyo kuumiza magoti hayo kulimaanisha kuharibu nguvu ([Kumbukumbu la Torati 28:35](https://ref.ly/Deut28:35)). Isaya alimwomba Mungu kufanya magoti dhaifu kuwa imara ([Isaya 35:3](https://ref.ly/Isa35:3)). Magoti dhaifu kwa kawaida yalionyesha ukosefu wa imani yenye nguvu ([Ayubu 4:4](https://ref.ly/Job4:4); [Waebrania 12:12](https://ref.ly/Heb12:12)). Wakati mwingine, hii ilimaanisha afya duni ([Zaburi 109:24](https://ref.ly/Ps109:24)). Ezekieli alizungumzia watu ambao magoti yao “yatageuka kuwa maji” ([Ezekieli 7:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:17); [21:7](https://ref.ly/Ezek21:7)).

Kupiga magoti mbele ya Mungu ilikuwa njia ya kuonyesha ibada ([Zaburi 95:6](https://ref.ly/Ps95:6)) na pia maombi ([Danieli 6:10](https://ref.ly/Dan6:10)). Yesu mwenyewe alipiga magoti kuomba katika bustani ya Gethsemane ([Luka 22:41](https://ref.ly/Luke22:41)). Petro, Paulo, na Stefano wote walifanya vivyo hivyo ([Matendo 7:60](https://ref.ly/Acts7:60); [9:40](https://ref.ly/Acts9:40); [20:36](https://ref.ly/Acts20:36); [21:5](https://ref.ly/Acts21:5)). Solomoni alipiga magoti katika maombi kwa Mungu ([1 Wafalme 8:54](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:54)). Wakati mmoja, hata alijenga jukwaa ili kila mtu aweze kumwona akipiga magoti mbele ya Mungu ([2 Mambo ya Nyakati 6:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr6:13)).

Wengine walipiga magoti kuonyesha huzuni kama Ezra alivyofanya wakati wa dhabihu ya jioni ([Ezra 9:5](https://ref.ly/Ezra9:5)). Petro alipiga magoti kuomba msamaha wa Bwana kwa ukosefu wake wa imani na uaminifu ([Luka 5:8](https://ref.ly/Luke5:8)). Watu waliokuwa wakimwomba nabii Eliya msaada walipiga magoti mbele yake kama mwakilishi wa Mungu ([2 Wafalme 1:13](https://ref.ly/2Kgs1:13)). Wengi walikuja wakipiga magoti na kumwomba Yesu kwa ajili ya uponyaji ([Mathayo 17:14](https://ref.ly/Matt17:14); [Marko 1:40](https://ref.ly/Mark1:40)). Danieli alipiga magoti kwa mshangao mbele ya malaika ([Danieli 10:10](https://ref.ly/Dan10:10)). Wakati Belshaza alikuwa na hofu, “magoti yake yaligongana” ([Danieli 5:6](https://ref.ly/Dan5:6)).

Kwenye Agano Jipya, Yesu alichekwa na askari. Walimtania na kujifanya kuwa yeye ni mfalme. Walipiga magoti mbele yake na kupiga kelele, “Salamu, Mfalme wa Wayahudi” ([Mathayo 27:29](https://ref.ly/Matt27:29); [Marko 15:19](https://ref.ly/Mark15:19)).
