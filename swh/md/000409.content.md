## Usingizi (id: 778887)

USINGIZI
========

Hali ya kawaida ya kupumzika kwa mwili na akili. Katika Biblia, usingizi unaweza kumaanisha mambo matatu:

1. kupumzika kwa mwili
2. kutofanya kazi kwa uadilifu au kiroho
3. kifo

Usingizi kama Pumziko la Mwili
------------------------------

Usingizi ambao mwili wa binadamu unahitaji unachukuliwa kama zawadi ya thamani kutoka kwa Mungu ([Zaburi 4:8](https://ref.ly/Ps4:8); [127:2](https://ref.ly/Ps127:2)). Usingizi unaweza kuzuiliwa, kama Mungu anavyochagua ili kutimiza makusudi yake ([Esta 6:1](https://ref.ly/Esth6:1); [Danieli 6:18](https://ref.ly/Dan6:18)). Mungu pia anaweza kuwapa watu usingizi mzito ([Mwanzo 2:21](https://ref.ly/Gen2:21); [15:12](https://ref.ly/Gen15:12); [1 Samweli 26:12](https://ref.ly/1Sam26:12)). Wakati mtu analala, Mungu anaweza kufanya mapenzi yake yajulikane kupitia ndoto au maono (kwa mfano, [Mwanzo 28:11–16](https://ref.ly/Gen28:11-Gen28:16); [Ayubu 4:13](https://ref.ly/Job4:13-Job4:17)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen28:11-Gen28:16)[17](https://ref.ly/Job4:13-Job4:17); [Mt 1:20](https://ref.ly/Matt1:20-Matt1:24)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen28:11-Gen28:16)[24](https://ref.ly/Matt1:20-Matt1:24)).

Kitabu cha Mithali kinaonya dhidi ya ukosefu wa nidhamu ya maisha unaoonyeshwa kwa kupenda sana usingizi. Kwa mfano, mithali moja inasema, "Usipende usingizi, usije ukawa maskini; fungua macho yako, nawe utakuwa na chakula kingi." ([Mithali 20:13](https://ref.ly/Prov20:13); tazama pia [6:9](https://ref.ly/Prov6:9-Prov6:11)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen28:11-Gen28:16)[11](https://ref.ly/Prov6:9-Prov6:11); [10:5](https://ref.ly/Prov10:5); [24:32](https://ref.ly/Prov24:32-Prov24:34)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen28:11-Gen28:16)[34](https://ref.ly/Prov24:32-Prov24:34)).

Usingizi kama Kutojibidiisha Kimaadili au Kiroho
------------------------------------------------

Kwa mfano, usingizi hutumiwa kumaanisha uvivu, uzembe, au kutofanya kazi. [Isaya 56:10](https://ref.ly/Isa56:10) inazungumzia wale ambao walishindwa katika jukumu lao kama viongozi wa watu wa Mungu: "Wao ni waotaji wanaolala, wakipenda kulala usingizi." Katika Agano Jipya, watumishi wa Bwana wanaitwa kuwa macho, ili Bwana wao atakapokuja asiwakute wamelala ([Marko 13:35](https://ref.ly/Mark13:35-Mark13:37)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen28:11-Gen28:16)[37](https://ref.ly/Mark13:35-Mark13:37); tazama pia [Mathayo 25:1](https://ref.ly/Matt25:1-Matt25:13)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen28:11-Gen28:16)[13](https://ref.ly/Matt25:1-Matt25:13); [26:40](https://ref.ly/Matt26:40-Matt26:46)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen28:11-Gen28:16)[46](https://ref.ly/Matt26:40-Matt26:46)). Changamoto ya kuwa macho kiroho inatokea katika barua za Agano Jipya:

* "Amka, wewe uliyelala, fufuka kutoka kwa wafu, na Kristo atakuangazia." ([Waefeso 5:14](https://ref.ly/Eph5:14)).
* "Kwa hiyo basi, tusilale kama wengine, bali tuwe macho na wenye kiasi." ([1 Wathesalonike 5:6](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:6)).

Usingizi Unavyofanana na Kifo
-----------------------------

Biblia mara nyingi huzungumzia kifo kama usingizi. Kwa kawaida katika Agano la Kale, mtu anapokufa, inasemekana amelala na baba zake (kwa mfano, [Kumbukumbu la Torati 31:16](https://ref.ly/Deut31:16); [2 Samweli 7:12](https://ref.ly/2Sam7:12)).

Yesu alizungumzia kifo kama usingizi ([Mathayo 9:24](https://ref.ly/Matt9:24); [Yohana 11:11](https://ref.ly/John11:11)). Vivyo hivyo, mtume Paulo alifanya hivyo ([1 Wakorintho 11:30](https://ref.ly/1Cor11:30); [15:20, 51](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:20,1Cor15:51); [1 Wathesalonike 4:13–14](https://ref.ly/1Thess4:13-1Thess4:14)). Baadhi ya kauli hizi zinaonyesha kwamba kifo ni hali ya muda. Hii ndiyo sababu inaitwa usingizi. Hata kitabu cha [Danieli 12:2](https://ref.ly/Dan12:2) kinasema kwamba kifo ni usingizi, hadi wafu "waamke, wengine kwa uzima wa milele, lakini wengine kwa aibu na dharau ya milele."

Sehemu zingine katika Agano Jipya ni maalum zaidi. Mafundisho kamili ya Biblia kuhusu maana ya kifo yanapaswa kujumuisha muktadha wa [Luka 23:43](https://ref.ly/Luke23:43), [2 Wakorintho 5:8](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:8), [Wafilipi 1:23](https://ref.ly/Phil1:23), na [1 Wathesalonike 5:13–14](https://ref.ly/1Thess5:13-1Thess5:14). Katika sura ya kwanza ya hizi, Yesu anamwambia mwizi anayekufa msalabani, "Leo utakuwa pamoja nami peponi." Katika sura ya pili, Paulo anazungumzia kifo kwake kama kwenda kuwa "nyumbani na Bwana."
