## Msamaha wa Dhambi (id: 689611)

MSAMAHA WA DHAMBI
=================

Fasihi ya Toleo la Mfalme Yakobo ina maana sawa na "msamaha wa dhambi." Agano Jipya linatumia maneno mbalimbali kuelezea ukweli huo mmoja. Maneno tofauti hutumiwa kuelezea dhana ya msamaha wa dhambi:

* "kupita juu" katika [Warumi 3:25](https://ref.ly/Rom3:25)
* "Imefunikwa" katika [Warumi 4:7](https://ref.ly/Rom4:7)
* "haijapokewa" katika [Warumi 4:8](https://ref.ly/Rom4:8)
* "sitazikumbuka tena" katika [Waebrania 10:17](https://ref.ly/Heb10:17)

Mojawapo ya njia muhimu zaidi za kuonyesha msamaha ni kupitia neno "msamaha" ([Mathayo 26:28](https://ref.ly/Matt26:28); [Marko 1:4](https://ref.ly/Mark1:4); [Luka 1:77](https://ref.ly/Luke1:77); [3:3](https://ref.ly/Luke3:3); [24:47](https://ref.ly/Luke24:47); [Matendo 2:38](https://ref.ly/Acts2:38); [10:43](https://ref.ly/Acts10:43); [Waebrania 9:22](https://ref.ly/Heb9:22); [10:18](https://ref.ly/Heb10:18)).

Msamaha una utamaduni wa kuvutia katika lugha ya Kigiriki. Katika muktadha wa kisheria, msamaha ulirejelea kuachishwa kazi, kuachiliwa kutoka kwa wajibu, au kufutwa kwa deni au adhabu. Kwa muda, ulianza kumaanisha msamaha au kuondolewa kodi.

Katika matumizi ya Agano Jipya, shughuli ya msamaha ina maana ya "kuachilia," "kuacha nyuma," au "kutuma mbali." Kwa sababu hii, neno hilo mara nyingi hutafsiriwa kama "msamaha" pamoja na "kusamehe dhambi" ([Matendo 5:31](https://ref.ly/Acts5:31); [13:38](https://ref.ly/Acts13:38); [26:18](https://ref.ly/Acts26:18); [Waefeso 1:7](https://ref.ly/Eph1:7); [Wakolosai 1:14](https://ref.ly/Col1:14)). Wakati msamaha unaweza kutolewa na Mungu na wanadamu, neno "msamaha" karibu kila mara linamaanisha msamaha unaotolewa na Mungu ([Mathayo 26:28](https://ref.ly/Matt26:28); [Matendo 10:43](https://ref.ly/Acts10:43)).

*Tazama pia* Msamaha.
