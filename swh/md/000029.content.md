## Barsaba, Barsaba (id: 468167)

BARSABA, BARSABBA
=================

Jina la mwisho la kibiblia. Barsabas linamaanisha "mwana wa Saba" katika Kiaramu. Barsabbas, "mwana wa Sabato," ndilo linatumika kama tahajia katika tafsiri za kisasa. Watu wawili katika Agano Jipya wana jina hili la mwisho: Yosefu Barsabbas na Yuda Barsabbas ([Matendo 1:23](https://ref.ly/Acts1:23); [15:22](https://ref.ly/Acts15:22)).

*Ona* Yosefu \#12; Yuda \#6.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 1:23; ACT 15:22
* **Associated Articles:** Yosefu (ID: `468192@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Yuda (ID: `468193@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
