## Zebedayo (id: 689627)

Zebedayo
========

Baba wa wanafunzi Yakobo na Yohana ([Mathayo 26:37](https://ref.ly/Matt26:37); [Marko 3:17](https://ref.ly/Mark3:17); [10:35](https://ref.ly/Mark10:35)). Zebedayo alikuwa mvuvi na inawezekana alikuwa tajiri kwani alikuwa na watumishi na uhusiano na Kuhani Mkuu ([Yohana 18:15](https://ref.ly/John18:15)). Ingawa yeye binafsi anaonekana mara moja tu katika simulizi ([Mathayo 4:21](https://ref.ly/Matt4:21); [Marko 1:19–20](https://ref.ly/Mark1:19-Mark1:20)), mke wake, Salome, anaonekana mara nyingi kama mmoja wa waumini waliomfuata Kristo.

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 4:21; MAT 26:37; MRK 3:17; MRK 10:35; JHN 18:15; MRK 1:19–MRK 1:20
