## Mtumishi (id: 689617)

MTUMISHI
========

Mtu aliyekuwa na wajibu wa kumtumikia bwana, ambaye pia angeweza kutoa kiwango fulani cha ulinzi. Watumishi wengine walikuwa watumwa chini ya kifungo cha kisheria, wakati wengine walikuwa watumishi kwa hiari. Sio kila mara inawezekana kutofautisha kati ya "mtumishi," "mtumwa," "mtumishi wa kiume," na "mtumishi wa kike." Maneno kadhaa katika Kiebrania na Kigiriki yametafsiriwa kama "mtumishi," ingawa tafsiri mpya wakati mwingine hupendelea maneno mengine.

Neno la Kiebrania kwa "kijana," "vijana," au "mvulana" mara nyingi linamaanisha mtumishi ([Kutoka 33:11](https://ref.ly/Exod33:11); [Hesabu 22:22](https://ref.ly/Num22:22); [2 Wafalme 4:12](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:12)). Neno linalomaanisha "mtumishi aliyezaliwa huru" lilirejelea watumishi wa Bwana, kama Walawi ([Ezra 8:17](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:17); [Isaya 61:6](https://ref.ly/Isa61:6); [Ezra 44:11](https://ref.ly/Ezek44:11)) au makuhani ([Kutoka 28:35](https://ref.ly/Exod28:35); [Yoeli 1:9](https://ref.ly/Joel1:9); [2:17](https://ref.ly/Joel2:17)). Wakati mwingine mawaziri wa mfalme wanaitwa watumishi ([1 Mambo ya Nyakati 27:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:1); [Mithali 29:12](https://ref.ly/Prov29:12)), kama vile malaika wanaohudumu mbele ya Bwana ([Zaburi 103:21](https://ref.ly/Ps103:21); [104:4](https://ref.ly/Ps104:4)). Mtumishi wa kuajiriwa au mfanyakazi wa mshahara pia alichukuliwa kuwa mtu huru ([Kutoka 12:45](https://ref.ly/Exod12:45); [Ayubu 7:1](https://ref.ly/Job7:1); [Malaki 3:5](https://ref.ly/Mal3:5)).

Neno la Kiebrania linalotumika mara nyingi zaidi, likitokea karibu mara 800 katika Agano la Kale, linamaanisha mtumwa aliyeshikiliwa katika kifungo ([Mwanzo 9:25](https://ref.ly/Gen9:25); [12:16](https://ref.ly/Gen12:16); [Kutoka 20:17](https://ref.ly/Exod20:17); [Kumbukumbu la Torati 5:15](https://ref.ly/Deut5:15); [15:17](https://ref.ly/Deut15:17)). Hata hivyo, neno hilo hilo linatumika kwa watu wa cheo cha heshima, kama vile mawaziri na washauri wa mfalme ([2 Wafalme 22:12](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:12); [2 Mambo ya Nyakati 34:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:20); [Nehemia 2:10](https://ref.ly/Neh2:10)) au mtumishi wa Mungu ([Mwanzo 24:14](https://ref.ly/Gen24:14); [Hesabu 12:7](https://ref.ly/Num12:7); [Yoshua 1:7](https://ref.ly/Josh1:7); [2 Wafalme 21:8](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:8)), katika maneno kama “Mose mtumishi wangu” \[au pia Daudi, Isaya, Israeli, Ayubu, na kadhalika]. Moja ya maneno yenye heshima zaidi ni “mtumishi wa Yahweh \[Bwana]” ([Kumbukumbu la Torati 34:5](https://ref.ly/Deut34:5); [Yoshua 1:13](https://ref.ly/Josh1:13); [8:31–33](https://ref.ly/Josh8:31-Josh8:33); [Isaya 49:1](https://ref.ly/Isa49:1-Isa49:6)–[6](https://ref.ly/Isa49:1-Isa49:6); [50:4](https://ref.ly/Isa50:4-Isa50:9)–[9](https://ref.ly/Isa50:4-Isa50:9); [52:13–53:12](https://ref.ly/Isa52:13-Isa53:12)). Jina sahihi Obadia linamaanisha “mtumishi wa Yahweh.”

Agano Jipya linaeleza mtumishi kwa njia mbalimbali kama mfanyakazi wa kuajiriwa au kibarua ([Marko 1:20](https://ref.ly/Mark1:20); [Luka 15:17](https://ref.ly/Luke15:17-Luke15:19)–[19](https://ref.ly/Luke15:17-Luke15:19); [Yohana 10:12](https://ref.ly/John10:12-John10:14)–[14](https://ref.ly/John10:12-John10:14)), kwa upana zaidi kama mtumwa ([Mathayo 8:9](https://ref.ly/Matt8:9); [10:24](https://ref.ly/Matt10:24-Matt10:25)–[25](https://ref.ly/Matt10:24-Matt10:25); [13:27](https://ref.ly/Matt13:27-Matt13:28)–[28](https://ref.ly/Matt13:27-Matt13:28); [Marko 10:44](https://ref.ly/Mark10:44); [12:2](https://ref.ly/Mark12:2-Mark12:4)–[4](https://ref.ly/Mark12:2-Mark12:4); [Luka 7:2](https://ref.ly/Luke7:2-Luke7:3,Luke7:8-Luke7:10)–[3, 8](https://ref.ly/Luke7:2-Luke7:3,Luke7:8-Luke7:10)–[10](https://ref.ly/Luke7:2-Luke7:3,Luke7:8-Luke7:10); [Yohana 4:51](https://ref.ly/John4:51); [8:34](https://ref.ly/John8:34); [13:16](https://ref.ly/John13:16); [Waefeso 6:5](https://ref.ly/Eph6:5); [Wakolosai 1:7](https://ref.ly/Col1:7)), na pia kama mtumishi wa nyumbani ([Luka 16:13](https://ref.ly/Luke16:13)).

*Tazama pia* Mtumwa na Utumwa.
