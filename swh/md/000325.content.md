## Rehema (id: 689599)

REHEMA
======

Rehema ni sifa maalum ya Mungu inayoonyesha upendo wake mwaminifu. Kupitia rehema, Mungu hutimiza ahadi zake kwa watu wake hata wanaposhindwa au kumwasi ([Kumbukumbu la Torati 30:1–6](https://ref.ly/Deut30:1-Deut30:6); [Isaya 14:1](https://ref.ly/Isa14:1); [Ezekieli 39:25–29](https://ref.ly/Ezek39:25-Ezek39:29); [Warumi 9:15–16, 23](https://ref.ly/Rom9:15-Rom9:16,Rom9:23); [11:32](https://ref.ly/Rom11:32); [Waefeso 2:4](https://ref.ly/Eph2:4)).

Kuelewa Rehema
--------------

Kwenye Biblia, rehema ina maana nyingi tofauti kwa sababu Kiebrania na Kigiriki (lugha asili za Biblia) zilitumia maneno kadhaa kuielezea. Maneno haya yanapotafsiriwa kwa Kiingereza, yanaweza kumaanisha mambo tofauti kama:

* Uzuri
* Upendo wa kweli (au kujitolea kwa upendo)
* Wema
* neema (wema wa Mungu ambao hatustahili)
* Mapendeleo
* Rehema
* Huruma (kuwajali wengine)
* Upendo usioweza kubadilika

Sehemu muhimu ya huruma ni kuwa na hamu ya kuwasamehe wale waliokosea na kuwasaidia wanapopitia matatizo.

Jinsi Mungu Anavyoonyesha Huruma
--------------------------------

Rehema inatokana na upendo wa Mungu kwa watu. Upendo huu unaonyeshwa kwa uhuru katika matendo yake ya kuwaokoa wale ambao ana ahadi nao kupitia uhusiano wake maalum wa agano. Katika Agano la Kale, alichagua watu wa Israeli kuwa wake na akawaonyesha rehema ([Kutoka 33:19](https://ref.ly/Exod33:19); [Isaiah 54:10](https://ref.ly/Isa54:10); [63:9](https://ref.ly/Isa63:9)). Mungu anaendelea kuwakubali watu wake wasiotii na daima anajaribu kuwarudisha kwake.

Mwenye zaburi anamwelezea Mungu kama Baba anayejali watoto wake wanaomheshimu na kumtumaini ([Zaburi 103:13](https://ref.ly/Ps103:13)). Hosea anaonyesha Mungu kama Baba mwenye upendo anayewatazama watu wake waasi kutoka mbinguni kwa uangalifu mkubwa ([Hosea 11](https://ref.ly/Hos11:1-Hos11:12); linganisha [Yeremia 31:20](https://ref.ly/Jer31:20)). Pia anaelezea Israeli kama mke asiye mwaminifu. Mungu anampenda Israeli kama mke wake mwaminifu licha ya uasi wa Israeli (kugeuka kutoka kwa Mungu) na dhambi ([Hosea 1–3](https://ref.ly/Hos1:1-Hos3:5); linganisha [Isaya 54:4–8](https://ref.ly/Isa54:4-Isa54:8)). Isaya anamwelezea Mungu kama mama anayejali mtoto wake ([Isaya 49:15](https://ref.ly/Isa49:15)).

Picha hizi zinaonyesha rehema za Mungu kwa njia mbalimbali. Vipengele vingine vya rehema za Mungu ni pamoja na Mungu kuwakubali watu wanaporudi kwake ([2 Wafalme 13:23](https://ref.ly/2Kgs13:23); [Isaya 54:8](https://ref.ly/Isa54:8); [Yoeli 2:18–32](https://ref.ly/Joel2:18-Joel2:32); [Mika 7:18–20](https://ref.ly/Mic7:18-Mic7:20)). Pia huwaokoa watu kutoka katika hali ngumu na hatari ([Nehemia 9:19–21](https://ref.ly/Neh9:19-Neh9:21); [Zaburi 40:11–17](https://ref.ly/Ps40:11-Ps40:17); [69:16–36](https://ref.ly/Ps69:16-Ps69:36); [79:8–9](https://ref.ly/Ps79:8-Ps79:9); [Isaya 49:10](https://ref.ly/Isa49:10)).

Kutokana na yale ambayo Waisraeli walijifunza kuhusu upendo na uaminifu wa Mungu, Wayahudi wanyofu waliomba rehema na msamaha wa Mungu walipohitaji. Hii inaonyeshwa kwa uzuri katika Zaburi za huzuni ([Zaburi 6](https://ref.ly/Ps6:1-Ps6:10); [32](https://ref.ly/Ps32:1-Ps32:11); [38](https://ref.ly/Ps38:1-Ps38:22); [51](https://ref.ly/Ps51:1-Ps51:19); [102](https://ref.ly/Ps102:1-Ps102:28); [130](https://ref.ly/Ps130:1-Ps130:8); [143](https://ref.ly/Ps143:1-Ps143:12)), pamoja na vifungu vingine vya Agano la Kale ([Kutoka 34:6](https://ref.ly/Exod34:6); [Nehemia 9:17](https://ref.ly/Neh9:17); [Zaburi 57](https://ref.ly/Ps57:1-Ps57:11); [79](https://ref.ly/Ps79:1-Ps79:13); [86](https://ref.ly/Ps86:1-Ps86:17); [123](https://ref.ly/Ps123:1-Ps123:4); [Isaiah 33:1–6](https://ref.ly/Isa33:1-Isa33:6); [Danieli 9:3–19](https://ref.ly/Dan9:3-Dan9:19); [Yoeli 2:13](https://ref.ly/Joel2:13)). Kukumbuka rehema ya Mungu kunawapa wale wanaojuta matumaini kwamba Mungu atawakubali tena.

Rehema katika Agano Jipya
-------------------------

Kwenye Agano Jipya, neno la Kigiriki lenye maelezo mengi linatumika kuelezea huruma ya Yesu kwa watu wenye uhitaji ([Mathayo 9:36](https://ref.ly/Matt9:36); [14:14](https://ref.ly/Matt14:14); [20:34](https://ref.ly/Matt20:34)). Linaonyesha huruma na kujali kwake kwa neno lenye nguvu linalomaanisha “kushtuka ndani ya matumbo.” Waebrania walifikiri matumbo au tumbo lilikuwa kitovu cha hisia, hasa wema. Yesu alionyeshwa kuwa na hisia za wema wa kina kwa wale wenye uhitaji na kutenda haraka kusaidia mateso yao. Aliponya ([Mathayo 20:34](https://ref.ly/Matt20:34); [Marko 1:41](https://ref.ly/Mark1:41)), alifufua wafu ([Luka 7:13](https://ref.ly/Luke7:13)), na alilisha wenye njaa ([Mathayo 15:32](https://ref.ly/Matt15:32)).

Wazo la Agano la Kale kuhusu rehema ya Mungu iliyoonyeshwa kwa watu wake waliochaguliwa pia linaonekana katika Agano Jipya ([Luka 1:50, 54, 72, 78](https://ref.ly/Luke1:50,Luke1:54,Luke1:72,Luke1:78); [Waefeso 2:4](https://ref.ly/Eph2:4); [1 Timotheo 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:2); [1 Petro 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:3); [2:10](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:10)). Matumizi ya kawaida ya rehema katika Agano Jipya ni mpango wa Mungu wa kuwaokoa watu kupitia Yesu Kristo ([Warumi 11:30–32](https://ref.ly/Rom11:30-Rom11:32); [Waefeso 2:4](https://ref.ly/Eph2:4)). Mungu ni “Baba wa rehema” ([2 Wakorintho 1:3](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:3)). Anawapa rehema wale wanaoamini katika Mwana wake. Ni kwa sababu yeye ni “tajiri wa rehema” kwamba aliwaokoa wale waliokufa kiroho na waliolaaniwa na dhambi zao ([Waefeso 2:4–6](https://ref.ly/Eph2:4-Eph2:6)). Ni kwa rehema ya Mungu kwamba mtu anasamehewa na kupewa Uzima wa milele ([1 Timotheo 1:13–16](https://ref.ly/1Tim1:13-1Tim1:16)).

Wajibu wa Watu Kuonyesha Huruma kwa Wengine
-------------------------------------------

Kwa sababu Mungu ametoa wema wake bure kwa wote, iwe ni wazuri au la, watu wanapaswa kuwa wema kwa wengine pia, hata kama hawastahili. Kwa kweli, watu wanahimizwa kuwa wema, hasa kwa wale ambao ni maskini, wanahitaji msaada, au wamepoteza wanafamilia ([Mithali 14:31](https://ref.ly/Prov14:31); [19:17](https://ref.ly/Prov19:17); [Mika 6:8](https://ref.ly/Mic6:8); [Zekaria 7:9–10](https://ref.ly/Zech7:9-Zech7:10); [Wakolosai 3:12](https://ref.ly/Col3:12)). Mungu anaona kuwa wema ni muhimu zaidi kuliko kufuata sheria au kutoa dhabihu ([Mathayo 9:13](https://ref.ly/Matt9:13)). Wema wa Mungu kupitia Yesu unamaanisha watu wanapaswa kuwatendea wengine jinsi Mungu amewatendea wao.

Yesu alifanya wema na msamaha kuwa sehemu kuu ya mafundisho yake ([Mathayo 5:7](https://ref.ly/Matt5:7); [9:13](https://ref.ly/Matt9:13); [12:7](https://ref.ly/Matt12:7); [23:23](https://ref.ly/Matt23:23); [Luka 6:36](https://ref.ly/Luke6:36); [10:37](https://ref.ly/Luke10:37); [Yakobo 3:17](https://ref.ly/Jas3:17)). Kabla ya kuja kwake, watu walizungumza kuhusu jinsi ambavyo angekuwa mwema na jinsi msamaha ungekuwa kitovu cha utume wake ([Luka 1:50, 54, 72, 78](https://ref.ly/Luke1:50,Luke1:54,Luke1:72,Luke1:78)).

Wakristo wanapaswa kujali na kusaidiana, kama Yesu alivyowasaidia. Wanapaswa kutoa msaada, upendo, na faraja kwa kila mmoja. Yakobo, mmoja wa wafuasi wa Yesu, anasema kwamba kufanya matendo mema ni sehemu muhimu sana ya imani ya kweli ([Yakobo 2:14–26](https://ref.ly/Jas2:14-Jas2:26)). Yesu alimsifu Msamaria mwema aliyekuwa na huruma. Msamaria mwema alimsaidia mtu aliyekuwa amejeruhiwa na kuibiwa ([Luka 10:36–37](https://ref.ly/Luke10:36-Luke10:37)). Kuwa na huruma ni sifa maalum ya watu wanaoishi katika ufalme wa Mungu ([Mathayo 5:7](https://ref.ly/Matt5:7)).
