## Mcha Mungu (id: 800590)

MCHA MUNGU
==========

Mtu anayeheshimu sana, kumwabudu, au kumwogopa Mungu. Hofu hii inaweza kuwa:

* Neno la heshima
* Hisia ya woga
* Hofu ya kuadhibiwa na Mungu

Kwenye Agano la Kale, misemo inayomwelezea mtu anayemcha Mungu mara nyingi huambatana na maneno kama "alisimama kwa hofu" au "alishikilia kwa hofu." Heshima kwa Bwana ni nadra lakini inatumika wakati Obadia alipowaficha manabii ili kuwaokoa kutoka kwa Yezebeli ([1 Wafalme 18:3–4, 12](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:3-1Kgs18:4)). Mtawala anayemcha Mungu alitarajiwa kutoa haki ([2 Samweli 23:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:3); [2 Mambo ya Nyakati 19:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr19:7)). Maisha marefu yalikuwa ahadi kwa wale waliomcha Bwana ([Mithali 10:27](https://ref.ly/Prov10:27); [14:27](https://ref.ly/Prov14:27); [19:23](https://ref.ly/Prov19:23)). Familia inayomcha Mungu ingemtegemea Bwana kwa msaada wakati wa shida ([2 Wafalme 4:1](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:1); [Mithali 14:26](https://ref.ly/Prov14:26)). Hofu ya Bwana ilikuwa na nguvu katika kufukuza dhambi na ilikuwa mwanzo wa hekima ([Hekima ya Solomoni 10:13](https://ref.ly/Wis10:13)).

Kwenye Agano Jipya, hofu ya Mungu mara nyingi inahusishwa na maagizo ya kumpenda na kumtumikia Bwana ([Wakolosai 3:22](https://ref.ly/Col3:22); [1 Petro 2:17](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:17)). Katika [Luka 1:50](https://ref.ly/Luke1:50), kauli ya Maria “Rehema zake zinawafikia wale wanaomcha” inamaanisha kumheshimu na kumtii. Katika Matendo, neno “Mcha Mungu” linarejelea watu wa mataifa ambao walihudhuria sinagogi. Paulo anawahutubia akisema: “Wanaume wa Israeli na ninyi watu wa mataifa mnaomcha Mungu” ([Matendo 13:16](https://ref.ly/Acts13:16)). Kornelio alikuwa jemadari wa Kirumi mwenye kumcha Mungu, aliyetambulika kama anaishi maisha yanayokubalika kwa Bwana, ingawa hakuwa Myahudi ([Matendo 10:2, 35](https://ref.ly/Acts10:2)).

Hofu ya Mungu pia inawakilisha woga au hofu ya nguvu na hukumu ya Mungu, kama inavyoonekana katika Agano la Kale na Jipya ([Mwanzo 3:10](https://ref.ly/Gen3:10); [Kumbukumbu la Torati 9:19](https://ref.ly/Deut9:19); [Ayubu 6:4](https://ref.ly/Job6:4); [9:28–29](https://ref.ly/Job9:28-Job9:29); [Zaburi 76:8](https://ref.ly/Ps76:8); [Mathayo 17:7](https://ref.ly/Matt17:7); [28:10](https://ref.ly/Matt28:10); [Luka 5:10](https://ref.ly/Luke5:10); [12:5](https://ref.ly/Luke12:5); [Matendo 5:5, 11](https://ref.ly/Acts5:5); [1 Timotheo 5:20](https://ref.ly/1Tim5:20)).

*Tazama pia* Hofu; Mabadiliko.
