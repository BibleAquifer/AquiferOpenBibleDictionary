## Henoko (Mtu) (id: 800583)

Enoki (Mwanadamu)
=================

1\. Mwana wa Kaini na mjukuu wa Adamu ([Mwa 4:17, 19](https://ref.ly/Gen4:17,Gen4:19)).

2\. Mwana wa Yaredi miongoni mwa wazao wa Sethi; baba wa Methusela ([Mwa 5:18–24](https://ref.ly/Gen5:18-Gen5:24); [1 Nya 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:3)). Aliishi katika uhusiano wa karibu sana na Mungu kiasi kwamba alichukuliwa mbinguni bila kufa.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 4:17; GEN 4:19; 1CH 1:3; GEN 5:18–GEN 5:24
