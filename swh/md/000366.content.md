## Talitha Cumi (id: 713746)

TALITHA KUMI
============

Yesu alizungumza maneno haya ya Kiaramu kama ilivyorekodiwa na Marko katika injili yake ([Marko 5:41](https://ref.ly/Mark5:41)). Yairo, afisa wa sinagogi katika eneo la Galilaya, alimwita Yesu ili amponye binti yake mgonjwa. Msichana alikufa kabla Yesu hajafika. Alipofika, Yesu alishika mkono wa msichana na kusema, *"Talitha cumi,"* ikimaanisha "Msichana mdogo, inuka." *"Talitha"* ni neno la upendo linalomaanisha "mwanakondoo" au "kijana." *"Cumi"* ni amri ya kuinuka. Marko alitafsiri neno hilo kama "Nakwambia, inuka!"

Kwenye injili yake, Marko anajumuisha baadhi ya misemo ya Kiaramu ambayo Yesu alizungumza ([Marko 3:17](https://ref.ly/Mark3:17); [5:41](https://ref.ly/Mark5:41); [7:11, 34](https://ref.ly/Mark7:11,Mark7:34); [11:9–10](https://ref.ly/Mark11:9-Mark11:10); [14:36](https://ref.ly/Mark14:36); [15:22, 34](https://ref.ly/Mark15:22,Mark15:34)). Mathayo anarekodi misemo miwili tu ya Kiaramu ([Mathayo 27:33, 46](https://ref.ly/Matt27:33,Matt27:46)). Injili ya Luka haijumuishi misemo hii.

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 27:33; MAT 27:46; MRK 3:17; MRK 5:41; MRK 7:11; MRK 7:34; MRK 14:36; MRK 15:22; MRK 15:34; MRK 11:9–MRK 11:10
