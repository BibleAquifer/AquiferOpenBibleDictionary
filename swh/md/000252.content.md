## Mtume, Utume (id: 468164)

MTUME, UTUME
============

Kichwa rasmi kilichopewa viongozi fulani katika makanisa ya Agano Jipya. Utume unahusu kazi na majukumu ya mtume. Watu wana mawazo tofauti kuhusu asili ya mitume wa Agano Jipya, walichofanya, na historia yao. Mila tofauti za kanisa hazikubaliani juu ya mambo haya. Ili kuelewa maneno "mtume" na "utume" vyema, tunaweza kuangalia asili ya maneno haya na mawazo yanayohusiana nayo.

Maneno "Mtume" na "Utume" Yalitoka Wapi?
----------------------------------------

Neno la Kigiriki kwa "mtume" halitumiki nje ya Agano Jipya kwa maana ile ile kama ilivyo katika Agano Jipya. Linatokana na neno linalomaanisha "kutuma." Mara nyingi hutumika wakati wa kujadili bahari, kumaanisha "meli" au "kikundi cha meli," "msafara wa baharini," au "kiongozi" wa msafara huo. Matumizi ya neno hili kwa kawaida ni yasiyo ya kibinafsi na yasiyo ya hiari, bila kuonyesha mpango wa kibinafsi au idhini. Papyri za baadaye, hati za kale zilizoandikwa kwenye nyenzo iliyotengenezwa kutoka kwa mmea uitwao papyrus, hutumia neno hilo kumaanisha "muswada," "ankara," au "pasipoti." Maneno haya pia hutumiwa na mabaharia.

Yesu Anachagua Mitume Wake Kumi na Wawili
-----------------------------------------

Kwenye Agano Jipya, "mtume" inahusu wale waliotumwa na Yesu kutangaza injili. Kutoka kwa kundi kubwa la wafuasi wake, Yesu alichagua wanaume kumi na wawili ([Mathayo 10:1](https://ref.ly/Matt10:1-Matt10:4)–[4](https://ref.ly/Matt10:1-Matt10:4); [Marko 3:13](https://ref.ly/Mark3:13-Mark3:19)–[19](https://ref.ly/Mark3:13-Mark3:19); [Luka 6:12](https://ref.ly/Luke6:12-Luke6:16)–[16](https://ref.ly/Luke6:12-Luke6:16)). Wanaume hawa kumi na wawili:

* alikuwa na uhusiano wa karibu naye,
* alipokea mafunzo ya kibinafsi, na
* alishuhudia miujiza yake na mabishano na viongozi wa Kiyahudi.

Yesu aliwatuma hawa kumi na wawili kwa:

* hubiri toba,
* fukuza mapepo,
* ponya wagonjwa, na
* hudumu kwa njia zinazofanana na kazi yake mwenyewe ([Mathayo 10:1](https://ref.ly/Matt10:1-Matt10:15)–[15](https://ref.ly/Matt10:1-Matt10:15); [Marko 6:7](https://ref.ly/Mark6:7-Mark6:13,Mark6:30)–[13, 30](https://ref.ly/Mark6:7-Mark6:13,Mark6:30); [Luka 9:1](https://ref.ly/Luke9:1-Luke9:6)–[6](https://ref.ly/Luke9:1-Luke9:6)).

Walimuakilisha Yesu binafsi, kama ilivyoelezwa katika msemo "Yeyote anayewasikiliza ninyi ananisikiliza Mimi; yeyote anayewakataa ninyi ananikataa Mimi; na yeyote anayenikataa Mimi anamkataa Yule aliyenituma" ([Luka 10:16](https://ref.ly/Luke10:16); linganisha [Mathayo 10:40](https://ref.ly/Matt10:40)). Wale Kumi na Wawili hawakupaswa tu kufundisha kile Yesu alichosema. Walikusudiwa pia kumwakilisha Yesu mwenyewe.

Baada ya ufufuo, Yesu aliwaagiza mitume kumi na wawili kutangaza tendo la Mungu katika Kristo kwa watu wote ([Mathayo 28](https://ref.ly/Matt28:1-Matt28:20); [Luka 24](https://ref.ly/Luke24:1-Luke24:53); [Yohana 20–21](https://ref.ly/John20:1-John21:25)). Ni wale tu ambao walikuwa na Yesu tangu mwanzo wa huduma yake hadi ufufuo wake ndio wangeweza kuwa mitume wake ([Matendo 1:21](https://ref.ly/Acts1:21-Acts1:22)–[22](https://ref.ly/Acts1:21-Acts1:22)). Paulo alistahili kwa sababu alikuwa amemwona Kristo aliyefufuka ([1 Wakorintho 15:4](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:4-1Cor15:10)–[10](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:4-1Cor15:10)).

Jinsi "Mtume" Inavyotumika Katika Maandishi ya Paulo
----------------------------------------------------

Kwenye maandishi ya Paulo, kuna matumizi mawili makuu ya neno "mtume":

* Wakati mwingine, Paulo hutumia neno "mtume" kumaanisha watu waliochaguliwa na makanisa ya eneo hilo kupeleka zawadi kwa Wakristo wengine kwa usalama ([2 Wakorintho 8:23](https://ref.ly/2Cor8:23); [Wafilipi 2:25\).](https://ref.ly/Phil2:25)
* Zaidi ya hayo, "mtume" hutumiwa kwa maana ya kiufundi na kifungu cha kufuzu "wa Yesu Kristo" ([1 Wakorintho 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:1); [2 Wakorintho 1:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:1); [11:13](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:13); [Wagalatia 1:1](https://ref.ly/Gal1:1); [Waefeso 1:1](https://ref.ly/Eph1:1); [Wakolosai 1:1](https://ref.ly/Col1:1); [1 Wathesalonike 2:6\).](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:6)

"Aliyetumwa" ni "aliyepelekwa wa Yesu Kristo" ([Warumi 16:7](https://ref.ly/Rom16:7); [1 Wakorintho 9:1, 5](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:1,1Cor9:5); [12:28](https://ref.ly/1Cor12:28); [Wagalatia 1:17](https://ref.ly/Gal1:17-Gal1:19)–[19](https://ref.ly/Gal1:17-Gal1:19)). Paulo anasema ana haki ya kuitwa mtume. Anategemea hili kwa mawazo yale yale kuhusu mitume ambayo Yesu alifundisha. Paulo daima anaunganisha dai lake na tukio maalum katika maisha yake ya zamani. Tukio hili lilikuwa wakati Yesu aliyefufuka alimtokea ([1 Wakorintho 9:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:1); [Wagalatia 1:12, 16](https://ref.ly/Gal1:12,Gal1:16)). Paulo alifikiri tukio hili lilikuwa muhimu kama nyakati ambazo Yesu alitokea kwa wengine baada ya kufufuka kwake ([1 Wakorintho 15:3](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:3-1Cor15:8)–[8](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:3-1Cor15:8)).

Paulo alidai uzoefu wake nje ya Dameski (linganisha [Matendo 9:1](https://ref.ly/Acts9:1-Acts9:19)–[19a](https://ref.ly/Acts9:1-Acts9:19); [22:6](https://ref.ly/Acts22:6-Acts22:16)–[16](https://ref.ly/Acts22:6-Acts22:16); [26:12](https://ref.ly/Acts26:12-Acts26:18)–[18](https://ref.ly/Acts26:12-Acts26:18); [Wagalatia 1:17](https://ref.ly/Gal1:17)) ulikuwa ni agizo la maisha yote kuhubiri Kristo aliyefufuka ([1 Wakorintho 1:17](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:17); [2:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:1-1Cor2:2)–[2](https://ref.ly/1Cor2:1-1Cor2:2)). Aliitwa kuhubiri zaidi kwa watu wa mataifa ([Matendo 9:15](https://ref.ly/Acts9:15); [22:15](https://ref.ly/Acts22:15); [26:17, 23](https://ref.ly/Acts26:17,Acts26:23); [Wagalatia 1:15](https://ref.ly/Gal1:15-Gal1:16)–[16](https://ref.ly/Gal1:15-Gal1:16)). Kupitia mahubiri yake, Kristo aliendelea kufanya kazi, akiumba watu wapya wa Mungu ([1 Wakorintho 9:1](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:1-1Cor9:2)–[2](https://ref.ly/1Cor9:1-1Cor9:2); [Wagalatia 2:8](https://ref.ly/Gal2:8)).
