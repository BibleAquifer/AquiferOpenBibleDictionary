## Uzima (id: 689595)

Uzima
=====

Kulingana na Biblia, uzima wote unatoka kwa Mungu Baba. Mungu anatoa uzima kupitia Mwana wake Yesu, ambaye alishiriki katika uumbaji wa kila kitu na kuwaokoa watu. Yesu anatoa uzima wa kweli kwa watu wote ([Yohana 6:57](https://ref.ly/John6:57)).

Baba Anayeishi
--------------

Mungu Baba yuko juu ya yote kama “Mungu aliye hai” ([Yeremia 10:10](https://ref.ly/Jer10:10); [Yohana 5:26](https://ref.ly/John5:26)). Mungu yuko hai na anafanya kazi. Mungu ni chanzo cha uzima wote ([1 Timotheo 6:13](https://ref.ly/1Tim6:13)). Mungu ndiye aliyepuliza pumzi ya uhai kwa wanadamu wakati wa uumbaji na anaendelea kuwahifadhi ([Ayubu 34:14](https://ref.ly/Job34:14-Job34:15)–[15](https://ref.ly/Job34:14-Job34:15)). Mungu anatoa uhai ([Mwanzo 17:16](https://ref.ly/Gen17:16)). Ni Mungu pekee anayeweza kuchukua uhai ([Mwanzo 3:22–24](https://ref.ly/Gen3:22-Gen3:24); [6:3](https://ref.ly/Gen6:3); [Zaburi 104:29](https://ref.ly/Ps104:29); [Luka 12:20](https://ref.ly/Luke12:20)).

Harakati ni ishara ya maisha. Biblia inatuambia kwamba wanadamu na wanyama wana pumzi ya uhai kutoka kwa Mungu. Yesu aliporudisha uhai kwa mtu aliyekufa, mtu huyo alianza kupumua na kuhamasika tena ([Luka 8:55](https://ref.ly/Luke8:55)). Katika lugha ya Kiebrania ya asili ya Biblia, [Mwanzo 1:24](https://ref.ly/Gen1:24) na [6:17](https://ref.ly/Gen6:17) inatuambia kwamba Mungu alitoa pumzi hii ya uhai kwa wanyama pia. Kwa hivyo, asili yote ina uhai unaotoka kwa Mungu ([Matendo 17:24](https://ref.ly/Acts17:24-Acts17:28)–[28](https://ref.ly/Acts17:24-Acts17:28)).

Kutokana na hili, maisha ni matakatifu. Hata hivyo, maisha ni mafupi kama nyasi, mawingu, umande, na kivuli ([1 Mambo ya Nyakati 29:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:15); [Ayubu 7:6, 9](https://ref.ly/Job7:6,Job7:9); [Yakobo 4:13–16](https://ref.ly/Jas4:13-Jas4:16); [1 Petro 1:24](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:24)). Maisha marefu yanavutia ([Mwanzo 35:29](https://ref.ly/Gen35:29)). Maisha yoyote ni bora kuliko kifo na yana thamani isiyo na kipimo ([Mhubiri 9:4–6](https://ref.ly/Eccl9:4-Eccl9:6); [Mathayo 6:25](https://ref.ly/Matt6:25); [16:26](https://ref.ly/Matt16:26)).

Waisraeli wa kale waliamini kwamba baada ya kifo, watu walikwenda mahali paitwapo Sheol. Walifikiri kwamba huko Sheol, watu waliishi bila kuhisi chochote, bila matumaini, na bila msaada wa Mungu ([Zaburi 88:3–12](https://ref.ly/Ps88:3-Ps88:12)).

Maisha yanakuwa bora wakati:

* watu wanampenda na kumtii Mungu ([Kumbukumbu la Torati 30:15–20](https://ref.ly/Deut30:15-Deut30:20); [1 Petro 3:8–12](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:8-1Pet3:12)),
* mungu huwasaidia watu katika nyakati ngumu ([Isaya 38:16](https://ref.ly/Isa38:16)), na
* Mungu huwabariki watu wake ([Mathayo 5:3–12](https://ref.ly/Matt5:3-Matt5:12)).

Kristo kama Uzima
-----------------

Neno la Kigiriki kwa "uzima" ni *zoe.* Katika Kigiriki cha jadi, linamaanisha uhai kwa ujumla. Maana hii hutumika mara chache katika Agano Jipya ([Matendo 17:25](https://ref.ly/Acts17:25); [Yakobo 4:14](https://ref.ly/Jas4:14); [Ufunuo 16:3](https://ref.ly/Rev16:3)). Katika hali nyingi, *zoe* inarejelea uzima wa milele wa kimungu—maisha ya Mungu ([Waefeso 4:18](https://ref.ly/Eph4:18)). Maisha haya yako ndani ya Kristo, na aliifanya ipatikane kwa wote wanaoamini.

Binadamu huzaliwa na maisha ya asili—yanayoitwa *psuche* kwa Kiyunani. *Psuche* inaweza kutafsiriwa kama "nafsi," "utu," au "maisha." Binadamu hawazaliwi na uzima wa milele. Maisha haya yanaweza kupatikana tu kwa kumwamini Yesu Kristo, ambaye ana uzima wa milele (*zoe*).

Yesu alionyesha uwezo wake wa kutoa uzima kwa njia nyingi. Watu waliona nguvu hii alipoongea kwa mamlaka na alipowagusa watu ili kuwaponya ([Mathayo 9:18](https://ref.ly/Matt9:18); [Marko 1:27, 41–42](https://ref.ly/Mark1:27,Mark1:41-Mark1:42); [5:27–29](https://ref.ly/Mark5:27-Mark5:29)). Yeye ni “Mwandishi wa uzima” ([Matendo 3:15](https://ref.ly/Acts3:15)). Anaonyesha watu jinsi ya kupata uzima wa kweli ([Mathayo 7:14](https://ref.ly/Matt7:14); [25:46](https://ref.ly/Matt25:46); [Marko 8:35–37](https://ref.ly/Mark8:35-Mark8:37); [9:42–47](https://ref.ly/Mark9:42-Mark9:47)).

Yesu alionyesha uwezo wake kwa kuwarudisha watu waliokufa kwenye uzima. Baada ya kufufuka kutoka kwa wafu, alikuwa “Roho anayetoa uzima,” akiwa na nguvu za “uzima usioweza kuharibiwa” ([Warumi 8:2](https://ref.ly/Rom8:2); [1 Wakorintho 15:45](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:45); [Waebrania 7:16](https://ref.ly/Heb7:16)). Kwa hivyo, Yesu ni uzima wetu ([Wakolosai 3:4](https://ref.ly/Col3:4)). Pamoja naye tunapata “upya wa uzima” ([Warumi 6:4](https://ref.ly/Rom6:4)). Sisi ni viumbe vipya na sasa tunaishi kwa ajili yake na si kwa ajili yetu wenyewe ([2 Wakorintho 5:15–17](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:15-2Cor5:17)).

Yohana anasisitiza kwamba Kristo ndiye chanzo cha maisha haya mapya ([Yohana 3:14–16](https://ref.ly/John3:14-John3:16); [5:21](https://ref.ly/John5:21)). Watu wanaomwamini Yesu wanakuwa watoto wa Mungu ([1:12](https://ref.ly/John1:12); [3:3, 5](https://ref.ly/John3:3,John3:5)). Wakati watu wanapomjua Mungu na Yesu, wanaanza kuishi maisha haya mapya sasa hivi ([Yohana 5:24](https://ref.ly/John5:24); [17:3](https://ref.ly/John17:3); [1 Yohana 5:11–12](https://ref.ly/1John5:11-1John5:12)). Tayari wamepokea maisha ya milele ([Yohana 10:28](https://ref.ly/John10:28); [11:26](https://ref.ly/John11:26)).

Maisha haya mapya huleta mambo mengi mazuri:

* Imejaa utajiri ([Yohana 10:10](https://ref.ly/John10:10))
* Inaleta ufahamu ([Yohana 8:12](https://ref.ly/John8:12))
* Inaleta uhuru na kuridhika ([Yohana 10:9](https://ref.ly/John10:9))
* Inasaidia watu kushinda dhambi ([Warumi 6:6–14](https://ref.ly/Rom6:6-Rom6:14))
* Inaleta amani na furaha ([Warumi 5:1–11](https://ref.ly/Rom5:1-Rom5:11))
* Ni kama kuwa na akiba isiyokauka ya maji safi ([Yohana 4:13–14](https://ref.ly/John4:13-John4:14); [7:37–38](https://ref.ly/John7:37-John7:38))
* Haikomi kamwe ([Yohana 5:24](https://ref.ly/John5:24); [1 Wakorintho 15:51–57](https://ref.ly/1Cor15:51-1Cor15:57)).

Haya yote yanawezekana kwa sababu tangu mwanzo, kila kilichokuja kuwa kilikuwa hai na uzima wake ([Yohana 1:4](https://ref.ly/John1:4)). Mungu Baba anatoa uzima kwa ulimwengu kupitia Yesu, ambaye pia ni chanzo cha uzima na anaweza kuutoa kwa yeyote anayemchagua ([Yohana 5:26](https://ref.ly/John5:26)). Yeye ni “ufufuo na uzima” ([Yohana 11:25](https://ref.ly/John11:25); [14:6](https://ref.ly/John14:6)). Yesu anaonyesha hili kwa kurejesha uzima kwa viungo vilivyopooza, kufufua wafu, na kushinda kifo kwa kufufuka ([Yohana 5:5–9](https://ref.ly/John5:5-John5:9); [11:43](https://ref.ly/John11:43); [20](https://ref.ly/John20:1-John20:31)). Watu hubaki katika kifo tu ikiwa wanakataa “kuja” kwa Yesu na “kuwa na uzima” ([Yohana 5:40](https://ref.ly/John5:40); linganisha [1 Yohana 3:14](https://ref.ly/1John3:14)).

*Tazama pia* Uzima wa Milele.
