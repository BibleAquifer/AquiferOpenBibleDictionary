## Wachungaji (id: 713706)

WACHUNGAJI
==========

Wanaume waliowatunza wanyama wa nyumbani, kama vile ng'ombe, kondoo na mbuzi ([Mwanzo 13:7–8](https://ref.ly/Gen13:7-Gen13:8); [26:20](https://ref.ly/Gen26:20); [1 Samweli 21:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam21:7)), pia walijumuisha wachungaji wa kondoo. Katika Agano Jipya, wachungaji wa kondoo walikuwa maarufu sana. Mfano wa mchungaji wa kondoo na kondoo wake ulitumiwa na Yesu ([Yohana 10:1–16](https://ref.ly/John10:1-John10:16)). Baadhi ya wachungaji walifuga nguruwe ([Mathayo 8:33](https://ref.ly/Matt8:33); [Marko 5:14](https://ref.ly/Mark5:14); [Luka 8:34](https://ref.ly/Luke8:34)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 26:20; 1SA 21:7; MAT 8:33; MRK 5:14; LUK 8:34; GEN 13:7–GEN 13:8; JHN 10:1–JHN 10:16
