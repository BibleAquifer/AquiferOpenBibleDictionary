## Mabaharia (id: 689614)

MABAHARIA
=========

Wanaume waliofunzwa kuendesha meli za baharini. Watu wa Israeli hawakusafiri mara kwa mara kwenye bahari kuu. Mabaharia na wavuvi wengi katika Israeli walifanya kazi kwenye Bahari ya Galilaya na Mto Yordani. Maandiko yanataja meli kubwa mara chache ([Mwanzo 49:13](https://ref.ly/Gen49:13); [Waamuzi 5:17](https://ref.ly/Judg5:17)). Solomoni alikuwa na kikosi cha meli huko Esion\-geberi kwenye Ghuba ya Aqaba ([1 Wafalme 9:26–28](https://ref.ly/1Kgs9:26-1Kgs9:28); [2 Mambo ya Nyakati 8:17–18](https://ref.ly/2Chr8:17-2Chr8:18); [9:21](https://ref.ly/2Chr9:21)). Yehoshafati pia alikuwa na kikosi huko Esion\-geberi ([1 Wafalme 22:48](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:48); [2 Mambo ya Nyakati 20:35–37](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:35-2Chr20:37)).

Agano Jipya mara nyingi linataja meli na mabaharia. Boti nyingi za uvuvi zilifanya kazi katika Galilaya ([Mathayo 14:22](https://ref.ly/Matt14:22); [Marko 1:19](https://ref.ly/Mark1:19); [3:9](https://ref.ly/Mark3:9); [Luka 5:2](https://ref.ly/Luke5:2); [Yohana 6:19, 22–24](https://ref.ly/John6:19,John6:22-John6:24); [21:8](https://ref.ly/John21:8)). Paulo alisafiri kwenda Rumi kwa meli kubwa ([Matendo 27:6–44](https://ref.ly/Acts27:6-Acts27:44)). Wafanyakazi wa meli au mabaharia wanatajwa katika [Matendo 27:27, 30](https://ref.ly/Acts27:27,Acts27:30). Neno "baharia" (Toleo la Mfalme Yakobo) linarejelea mabaharia ([Ezekieli 27:9, 27–29](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:9,Ezek27:27-Ezek27:29); [Yona 1:5](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:5)).
