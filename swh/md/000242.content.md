## Moyo (id: 689583)

Moyo
====

Kiungo muhimu cha mwili; kitovu cha hisia za mtu.

Katika Kiebrania na Kigiriki, kama ilivyo katika Kiingereza cha kisasa, "moyo" hutumika kuashiria kiungo cha mwili pamoja na kituo cha hisia cha mtu. "Moyo" (Kiebrania leb*;* Kigiriki kardia) hutokea takriban mara 1,000 katika Biblia, ingawa mara nyingi hufichwa katika tafsiri. Wigo wa maana ni mpana.

Moyo wa Kimwili
---------------

Kwamba moyo unaopiga unaashiria uhai inaonekana kudokezwa katika [1 Samweli 25:37–39](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:37-1Sam25:39) (tazama nlt mg), licha ya kuchelewa kwa kifo cha Nabali. Chakula cha kimwili na divai huathiri moyo ([Amu 19:5](https://ref.ly/Judg19:5); [Zab 104:15](https://ref.ly/Ps104:15); [Matendo 14:17](https://ref.ly/Acts14:17)), na moyo unaweza "kufa " na "kutetemeka." Nafasi ya moyo katika mwili inatoa sitiari dhahiri kwa "katikati" ([Mt 12:40](https://ref.ly/Matt12:40)).

Moyo wa kisaikolojia
--------------------

Moyo unafanya kazi kiakili (kwa mfano, [Yer 12:11](https://ref.ly/Jer12:11)); pia unaona ([Yn 12:40](https://ref.ly/John12:40)), unaelewa ([1 Fal 3:9](https://ref.ly/1Kgs3:9)), unajadili ([Mk 2:6](https://ref.ly/Mark2:6)), unafikiri ([Lk 2:19](https://ref.ly/Luke2:19)), unakumbuka ([Lk 2:51](https://ref.ly/Luke2:51)), unawaza ([Kum 8:17](https://ref.ly/Deut8:17)), unafikiria ([Lk 1:51](https://ref.ly/Luke1:51)), una hekima ([Mhu 1:17](https://ref.ly/Eccl1:17), kjv), una ujuzi wa kiufundi ([Kut 28:3](https://ref.ly/Exod28:3), kjv), na mengine mengi.

Kihisia, moyo hupitia furaha ya kulewa ([1 Sm 25:36](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:36)), furaha ([Is 30:29](https://ref.ly/Isa30:29)), shangwe ([Yn 16:22](https://ref.ly/John16:22)), huzuni ([Neh 2:2](https://ref.ly/Neh2:2)), maumivu ([Rum 9:2](https://ref.ly/Rom9:2)), uchungu ([Met 14:10](https://ref.ly/Prov14:10)), wasiwasi ([1 Sm 4:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam4:13)), kukata tamaa ([Mhu 2:20](https://ref.ly/Eccl2:20)), upendo ([2 Sm 14:1](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:1)), imani ([Zab 112:7](https://ref.ly/Ps112:7)), mapenzi ([2 Kor 7:3](https://ref.ly/2Cor7:3)), tamaa ([Mt 5:28](https://ref.ly/Matt5:28)), ukatili ([Mk 3:5](https://ref.ly/Mark3:5)), chuki ([Law 19:17](https://ref.ly/Lev19:17)), hofu ([Mwa 42:28](https://ref.ly/Gen42:28)), wivu ([Yak 3:14](https://ref.ly/Jas3:14)), tamaa ([Rum 10:1](https://ref.ly/Rom10:1)), kukatishwa tamaa ([Hes 32:9](https://ref.ly/Num32:9)), huruma ([Kut 23:9](https://ref.ly/Exod23:9)), hasira ([Kum 19:6](https://ref.ly/Deut19:6), kjv), kutokuwa na uhakika ([2 Nya 13:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr13:7), kjv), na hisia nyingine nyingi.

Kwa hiari, moyo unaweza kukusudia ([1 Kor 4:5](https://ref.ly/1Cor4:5)), kuelekea ([1 Sam 14:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam14:7)), kuchochea ([2 Fal 12:4](https://ref.ly/2Kgs12:4); cf. [Mith 4:23](https://ref.ly/Prov4:23)), kuwa imara ([Matendo 11:23](https://ref.ly/Acts11:23)), kuwa na nia ([Kut 35:22](https://ref.ly/Exod35:22)), kubuni uovu ([Matendo 5:4](https://ref.ly/Acts5:4)), au kufuata “hazina” yake ([Mt 6:21](https://ref.ly/Matt6:21)).

Kimaadili, moyo unaweza kuwa mpole na mnyenyekevu ([Mt 11:29](https://ref.ly/Matt11:29)), mtakatifu ([1 Thes 3:13](https://ref.ly/1Thess3:13)), mwaminifu ([Neh 9:8](https://ref.ly/Neh9:8)), mnyofu ([Ps 97:11](https://ref.ly/Ps97:11)), safi, na mwenye nia moja ([Jas 4:8](https://ref.ly/Jas4:8)), safi ([Matendo 15:8](https://ref.ly/Acts15:8)), wenye upendo kwa Mungu ([Mk 12:30](https://ref.ly/Mark12:30)) na kwa wengine ([1 Pt 1:22](https://ref.ly/1Pet1:22)), mgumu, au nyeti ([Ez 11:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek11:19)). Maandiko yanasisitiza uovu wa moyo ([Gn 6:5](https://ref.ly/Gen6:5) na kote), jinsi unavyojiongopea ([Yak 1:26](https://ref.ly/Jas1:26)), udanganyifu wake ([Jer 17:9](https://ref.ly/Jer17:9)), uchoyo ([Mt 6:19–21](https://ref.ly/Matt6:19-Matt6:21)), tamaa ([Mt 5:28](https://ref.ly/Matt5:28)), kiburi ([Is 9:9](https://ref.ly/Isa9:9)), kutokuwa na heshima ([Matendo 7:51](https://ref.ly/Acts7:51)), upotovu ([Ps 101:4](https://ref.ly/Ps101:4)), na kutokuwa na toba ([Rom 2:5](https://ref.ly/Rom2:5)). Hakuna kinachomchafua mtu zaidi ya moyo wake mwenyewe ([Mk 7:18–19](https://ref.ly/Mark7:18-Mark7:19)).

Hata hivyo, kutoka moyoni kunaweza kutoka mema ([Lk 6:45](https://ref.ly/Luke6:45); [8:15](https://ref.ly/Luke8:15)). Hata wakati wa kukatishwa tamaa na hali au kwa hofu, nia njema ya moyo inabaki kuwa njema; na nia mbaya inabaki kuwa mbaya ([1 Fal 8:18](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:18); [Mt 5:28](https://ref.ly/Matt5:28)).

Inakuwa vigumu sana, moyo wa mtu kwa bahati mbaya umegawanyika, na Maandiko mara nyingi husifu moyo mkamilifu, mzima, na wa kweli (yaani, ulioungana) ([Mwa 20:5](https://ref.ly/Gen20:5); [Zab 86:11](https://ref.ly/Ps86:11); [Matendo 8:37](https://ref.ly/Acts8:37)). "Moyo" unaashiria nafsi ya ndani kabisa, kiini cha siri cha mtu ([1 Pet 3:4](https://ref.ly/1Pet3:4)), ambacho mtu huwasiliana nacho, na ambacho mtu "humimina" katika maombi, maneno, na matendo ([Zab 62:8](https://ref.ly/Ps62:8); [Mt 15:18–19](https://ref.ly/Matt15:18-Matt15:19)). Ni nafsi halisi, inayotofautishwa na mwonekano, nafasi ya umma, na uwepo wa kimwili ([1 Sam 16:7](https://ref.ly/1Sam16:7); [2 Kor 5:12](https://ref.ly/2Cor5:12); [1 Thes 2:17](https://ref.ly/1Thess2:17)). Na "nafsi ya moyo" hii ina asili yake, tabia, na mwelekeo ([Dan 4:16](https://ref.ly/Dan4:16); [7:4](https://ref.ly/Dan7:4), kjv; cf. [Mt 12:33–37](https://ref.ly/Matt12:33-Matt12:37)).

Moyo wa Kiroho
--------------

Moyo ni muhimu sana katika dini ya Kibiblia. Siri ya nafsi iliyofichwa inajulikana kikamilifu na Mungu na Kristo ([Yer 17:10](https://ref.ly/Jer17:10); [Lk 9:47](https://ref.ly/Luke9:47); [Rum 8:27](https://ref.ly/Rom8:27)), na moyo ni kiti cha maarifa yetu ya Mungu ([2 Kor 4:6](https://ref.ly/2Cor4:6)). Hali ya moyo inaongoza maono ya Mungu ([Mt 5:8](https://ref.ly/Matt5:8)); kutoka moyoni mtu huzungumza na Mungu ([Zab 27:8](https://ref.ly/Ps27:8)); moyo ni mahali pa makao ya Kimungu ([2 Kor 1:22](https://ref.ly/2Cor1:22); [Gal 4:6](https://ref.ly/Gal4:6); [Efe 3:17](https://ref.ly/Eph3:17)).

Kwa upande mwingine, uovu wa kimaadili moyoni unaonekana kama dhambi dhidi ya Mungu. Mioyo isiyo na hisia imefunikwa na giza, mara nyingi ikiwa na ibada ya siri, mbali na Mungu, na "sio sawa" mbele ya Mungu ([Kumb 29:18–19](https://ref.ly/Deut29:18-Deut29:19); [Mt 15:8](https://ref.ly/Matt15:8); [Matendo 8:21](https://ref.ly/Acts8:21); [Rum 1:21](https://ref.ly/Rom1:21)). Hata hivyo, Bwana hataidharau moyo uliovunjika na wenye toba ([Zab 51:17](https://ref.ly/Ps51:17)). Moyo wa mtu unapogeuka kuelekea Mungu, anaahidi kuufanya uwe na hisia kwa mambo ya kiungu, upya na safi ([Kumb 4:29](https://ref.ly/Deut4:29); [2 Fal 23:25](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:25); [Zab 51:10](https://ref.ly/Ps51:10); [Yoeli 2:13](https://ref.ly/Joel2:13); [Eze 36:25–27](https://ref.ly/Ezek36:25-Ezek36:27)). Sheria ya Mungu itakuwa imeandikwa moyoni kama mwongozo wa ndani na msukumo ([Yer 31:33](https://ref.ly/Jer31:33); [Ebr 8:10](https://ref.ly/Heb8:10); cf. [2 Kor 3:2–3](https://ref.ly/2Cor3:2-2Cor3:3)).

Kwa maneno ya Kikristo, mabadiliko kama hayo yanahusisha kuamini injili kutoka kwa "moyo mwaminifu na mwema" ambao unatoa udongo wenye rutuba kwa Neno la Mungu ([Lk 8:15](https://ref.ly/Luke8:15); [Rom 10:9](https://ref.ly/Rom10:9)). Moyo wa kweli unamkaribia Mungu, unampenda kwa akili yake yote, hisia, na mapenzi ([Lk 10:27](https://ref.ly/Luke10:27); [Heb 10:22](https://ref.ly/Heb10:22)). Kisha Mungu anatoa nguvu, thawabu, upya, neema, amani, na furaha kwa moyo ([Ps 73:26](https://ref.ly/Ps73:26); [Is 57:15](https://ref.ly/Isa57:15); [Matendo 2:46](https://ref.ly/Acts2:46); [Phil 4:7](https://ref.ly/Phil4:7); [Heb 13:9](https://ref.ly/Heb13:9)). Hivyo basi, wazo la kale linawezekana tena, lile la kuwa "mtu baada ya moyo wa Mungu" ([1 Sm 13:14](https://ref.ly/1Sam13:14); [Matendo 13:22](https://ref.ly/Acts13:22)).
